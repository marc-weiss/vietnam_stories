Honor the resister. Good idea, but don't crucify the soldier. Not everyone was as privileged as you must have been to decide not to go. Many people went into the war because they had no viable alternative.

As one who protested from 1964 until the end of the war, as one who made the protest of the war a single focus of that period I never blamed the vets. Your memorial is in the fact that lives were saved, a war ended.
