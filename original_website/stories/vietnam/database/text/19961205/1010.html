we the enemy:

im my bunker staring out the small window i sat in the dark looking...searching for what!..for whom!..the enemy? who are they?..where are they?..why are they? am i the enemy? who came in search of the enemy, through an enemys eyes. down in the rice paddies, they worked and hid, straw hats prtecting them from the sun, shielding their eyes from my eyes, the enemys eyes? it was easy for them to see i was the enemy, but it was hard for me to see, if they were the enemy, harder for me to decide, which one of us were the enemy,

i couldn't be the enemy, i came to free them, but instead,

i freed myself....

the man behind the pen...
