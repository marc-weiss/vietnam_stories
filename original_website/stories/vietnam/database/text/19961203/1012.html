The most memorable experience—I remember we would sit on Sunday evening in the Student Union and watch television and I think it was there when we were all sitting around watching the Smothers Brothers on Sunday night and Nixon came on and talked about, with these charts, bombing Cambodia. I think this was in 1970, because that is what led to the protests at Kent State, which then led to the shootings at Kent State and also at that time I was getting married.

David Hurwitz

Dayton, OH

As told at the Dayton Story Circle
