Today, I head a program which stands besides and supports all our servicemen and women. Since l968 I have dedicated my life to this program. Today it is called America Supporting Americans. My brother Sgt.Joseph Artavia started this program when he asked One City to Adopt his Unit for Morale Support. This request although he was KIA two weeks later has span over three decades and supported troops through Vietnam, Gulf, Somalia,Haiti,and Today. His story, and history our written on website http://www.asa.theveteran.net It is a story that effected hundreds of members of the l0lst Airborne Division and his own unit Co.A, l/327th. My life, my values, my relationships, my attitude towards life or death change when I spent Christmas of l968 with my brothers buddy's and the Unit we adopted in the fields of Vietnam.
