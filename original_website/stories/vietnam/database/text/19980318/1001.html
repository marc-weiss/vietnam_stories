My father was in the Vietnam War. An officer came to our house to tell us that my father was killed. My mom took it very hard and I was also upset but the pers on that took it the hardest was my sister, Natasha. When my mom told her she refused to talk to anyone and she also refused to eat. She spent most of her days looking at pictures of her dad as she cried. Due to her refusal to eat, she became very sick. My mom didn't realize how badly it was effecting her because she worked alot. She would come home and have more work to do that she brought home. One day I went upstairs into her room. She was very pale and was very tired. I asked her if she was alright but she couldn't speak. She didn't have the strength to stand up and could barely open her eyes. I could tell that something was wrong so I called my mom. When she came home she picked her up and took her to the hospital but it was too late there was nothing that the doctors could do. After Natasha's death, my mom dropped me off at a friend's house and never returned. I was then put in Foster care and in one month I was in four different foster homes. I had lost all hope. Then people taught me about GOD and my spirits were lifted up. I felt like I had no friends but at least I had a spiritual father. My dad,Craig McGregory told me that he would teach me all about planes one day but he was never able to. Alot of times I look at the sky and the Sun and cry. I think "Why can't their be world peace, why did my dad have to be killed didn't people know that I loved him." I try to be happy but how can a person be happy when they don't know how long they will be with a family before being moved again. Nobody can understand how I feel being the second in everything. I think why didn't my mom love me. I have come to hate guns and violence for that is what killed my dad but now I am alone. If I do something bad I get screamed at but if I do something good nobody cares. I am afraid to make friends because there were three people that I cared about. My mom didn't love me and my dad and sister were killed. I don't want anyone else to die so I won't get close to them. When ever I have a problem I deal with it on my own but if I had only one wish it would be that my dad be alive and that there would be world peace but until GOD is in more people's hearts I guess families will continue to be broken and people will love to kill even if it hurts little kids justs so they can get there way. I wish that I could put Jesus in people's hearts but I guess that could only happen if people would allow Jesus to come in so the world will always fight right? But why?If you knew all the people it hurt or the way my life has been changed then you would see my view. I feel sorry for the world but I alone can't change anything. It would take alot of Christians but most of all it would mean that you would have to change but we could never make you. I hope you will never have to go through what I went through. I can't understand why people wanted to kill my dad because he was so kind. He probably had no fight left in him and neither do I. So if you want to kill me then go ahead I won't stop you. Besides what is the sense of living on an earth where there is no peace and everyone is always fighting and killing? If you were me wouldn't you want to be dead instead of alive? I am tired of fighting and watching people get hurt I will stop now let the world do what they want to me Just if you could please think of all the people you hurt when you say things Verbaly and Physically. I can only ask that the world stops fighting but I can't force anyone nor will I try but if you could only understand what I've been through then you would stop fighting with you mouths and fists and you would surrender to Jesus and fight for him instead of against him. Well I won't take up any morte of your time.

Crystal Rose
