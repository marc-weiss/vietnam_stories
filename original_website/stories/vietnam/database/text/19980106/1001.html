I served in Vietnam from June '68 until June '69 with the 7/17th Air Cavalry at Plieku, VN with the 4th Infantry Division. I was a CWO helicopter pilot and flew Cobra gunships. I returned for a second tour of duty from February to November 1971 with the 57th Aviation Company again at Plieku, VN with the ARVNs. I was a platoon commander (Captain) and flew Cobra gunships. Many returned from VN and felt guilty because we did not "win" the war. I felt guilty because I came to believe that the war was immoral. For eight years, I had nightmares and drank too much alcohol. In 1982 I joined a 12 step program and began my spiritual recovery. I have written many pages about my experience and have submitted some of my writings to Maxine Hong Kingston for publication in her anthology.
