Sons of the Revolution

Sons of the Revolution! Men of the New World! Offering a New Hope to the World's hopeless! Look at what your brought to Earth's optimistic multitudes: "Millions for defense, Not one cent for tribute!: "We have met the enemy and they are outs!" "Remembe the Alamo!" "Fifty-four, forty or fight!" "Remember the Maine!" "Damn the torpedoes, Full speed ahead!" "Remember Pearl Harbor!"

The inheritors of the fruit of the Revolution Made the light at Hiroshima and the heat of Nagasaki, Bringing into question the intentions of the Seat of Liberty!

It is sad to witness the death of Hope. The sons of the Revolution killed the hopes of the World at My Lai as they gave the lie to the American Dream! They buried it beneath the bodies of the women and children lying atop the tangled vine twisted spaces of the defenseless' thatched huts!

The Sons of the Revolution, come to spread the message of the Fathers of the Revolution: "When in the course of human events, it become necessary for one People to dissolve the Political Bands which have connected them with another..." The Sons of the Revolution saw to the final dissolution of the bands connecting their People with those they came to set free!

The Sons of the Revolution delivered their message underscored hotly with chunks of lead and denied with their spitting muzzles the truth of their Father's Revolution: "that all Men are created equal..." denying to these People of Earth "Life," "Liberty," "and the Pursuit of Happiness."

The Fathers of the Revolution appealed proudly to: "The Supreme Judge of the World In the Rectitude of our Intentions."

The Sons of the Revolution rebelled against these intentions and have earned the hate and suspicion of the assemblage of man!

The Fathers of the Revolution pledged before the World their "Sacred Honor."

The Sons of the Revolution desecrated their honor before the Court of the World!

Bob Hickey
1934
Tucson, AZ USA
