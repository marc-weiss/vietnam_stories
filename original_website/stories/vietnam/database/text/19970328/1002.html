I joined the Army right after high school in 1970.I did'nt have a draft number yet.I knew full well I'd end up going to Viet Nam but my dad was a paratrooper in WW 2 and we were always raised with a great respect for our country.I still have that respect for our country but the war opened my eyes to the way things are......hard to explain.We ( the soldiers) tried to help the South Vietnamese but this was a war directed by politics so they did'nt let us win.I remember the flack about the Wall when it was built .I was on a bus tour from church to D.C. with my wife and two small children when we pulled up to visit the Wall.I ran ahead by myself and stopped midway......I was overwelmed with tears as I saw all those names........all those people.........gone.But we will never let them be forgotten.The monument couldn't be more fitting.
