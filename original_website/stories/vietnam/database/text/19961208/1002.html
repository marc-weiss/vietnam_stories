Vietnam Story

We were teachers in northern Greece in l965-l966 at an American school, and were summer-traveling in Zurich,Switzerland when we accidentally drove into a pedestrian-only park. A little old Swiss lady,waving her umbrella,stepped in our path to stop our car. When she realized we were English-speaking Americans,she began to harangue us in broken English about the Viet Nam War and why we were fighting the poor miserable people there. Her passionate feelings made us reevaluate the assumptions we'd made about this at-the-time vague war from our months'old reading of Time magazines mailed to us from the States.

Bette Mackey
