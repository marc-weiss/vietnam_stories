I was born in Saigon August 8, 1969 and left May 1974. I was adopted by an American family in Camp Zama, Japan. I want to find my mother and if possible my father, who was a soldier. I was blessed to have a mother who loved me enough to give me up for adoption, so that I can live a better life. I am now happily married with one child, but I still feel that I have a piece of myself missing. My mother's name was Nguyen Xihn Thi and my birth name was Nguyen Thi Linn. I also have a brother. I have no info of my father. I just want my mother to know that I am doing well and that she has a handsome/very smart grandson. Also, that I love her very much. I hope and pray that one day that we will be reunited.

Thy Lynn Lewis
1969
Arlington, Texas
