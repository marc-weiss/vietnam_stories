I served with a Trans Bn, at Phu Loi, Nov 67- nov 68. I was assigned to the s security platoon. One morning while standing outside the bunker with 2 or 3 others, I wanted to burn the trash in the 55 gal drum. I got the trash burning, and was standing next to it. There was a loud pop, and I felt something hot hit my face. I reached up and felt my face, only to find peanut butter on my face. What a relief. Had the can of peanut butter been on top of the trash, I would have been hit by shrapnel. After that, I no longer burned trash.

Bruce Carbone
1948
Mulberry, Fl USA
