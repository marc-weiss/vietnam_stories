ALIVE THROUGH LOVE

Life is meant to be enjoyed not just lived. Life is a time in which learning, understanding and happiness must all be present at the same moment. Life is life only if you can feel it in your heart and soul concurrently. In most cases that is how it is. But sometimes things happen within ones life that forces the two, heart and soul, to separate. Being separated makes it impossible to be able to feel with both simultaneously. Results, alive but without life.

It was in this state of mind of living that I spent a major portion of my life. I went through a very dark period preceding this time where I did, saw, smelled, felt and heard things that should not be. Yes, war is hell and everyone who has ever been at war knows the horrors and the memories of things so vile they tend to twist the mind. I wish that I were one of those people. I wish that I had simple gone to war. I was not drafted, I was not sent to Vietnam, I volunteered. I wanted to go. I wanted to experience the glory and adventure of war. I thought it would make me a real man. Vietnam being Vietnam, distorted my mind and allowed me to do things that I can not believe I did now that I can think back about such things. If I were to tell you some of the things that I have done to other people, your skin would crawl. But worse, if I told you what I was thinking about as I did these things, it would terrorize your thoughts. I know living with these memories for the last 27 years, has ripped my brain apart. It would seem that I was almost constantly in some kind of mental struggle within myself; A struggle that overwhelmed everything and everyone around me. I look back now and I can see that the time I was struggling with myself, I was not a very easy person to get along with. Drinking too much, taking drugs trying to make the demons I had created to go away. But the harder I tried, the worse they became. The worse they became, the worse I got!

Between 1970 and 1995, I went through four marriages that all ended in disaster. I know that I hurt a lot of people and played a very big part in the outcomes, but I will not take all the blame. Now matter how bad the dancing, it always takes two to tango. But that is of no importance anymore. That period of time in which my life seemed to be nothing more then existing moment to moment is only a recalled thought with the same feelings you get when you recall a gruesome scene from a movie you saw or a book you read. I can think it, but I can no longer feel it. It is like that portion of my life did not exist!

Dr. Frankenstine yelled, ōItÆs alive!!ö when his creation stirred for the first time. Well I can now say, ōIÆm alive!!ö for I once again feel with my heart and soul at the same time. The feelings that my life has meaning and worth have been returned to me. The feelings of having someone love me with all their heart and soul, so pure and unsolicited has entered my life for the first time.

Allow me to tell you how the feelings of life were put back into me by a very beautiful oriental woman who now has the name of, Priny M. Boutcher.

I feel like I am a kid. I feel young and new. I feel like all that has past, never did. I feel truly ōaliveö. Literally, I feel as thought all I can remember is not my memory. I am at peace within myself. Nothing bothers me. I do not get mad. I donÆt loose my temper. I donÆt get nasty. I donÆt fight. I donÆt feel the need to fight. I donÆt drink, I do not take drugs, I donÆt get nuts when someone does something really stupid when driving. I am not the monster that I remember I was. As a matter of fact, I have this very strange feeling that I was born again on August 15th 1995 at 7:00 PM.

I remember the moment I first saw Priny. Before even talking to her, I felt something deep inside me stir. But at the time my circumstance was not a very good one so I dismissed the feelings and thought of them no longer. But if I had had a wish at that moment, I would have made it to be that this vision should become part of my life. Maybe there are Genies here is Saudi Arabia. . . My wish certainly came true!

I can honestly say that Priny was never the cause of anything that happened concerning my life that was left back in New York. That had fallen apart all by itself a long time ago. One of the major reasons I came to Saudi was to escape from an existence that offered nothing but mental torment trying to deal with myself as well as a self centered, domineering and inconsiderate individual. IÆm not sure if I had any intention of ever coming back when I first came to Saudi. I will say this, I tried to revive the beginning with letters expressing my feelings. But I never got one in return. Only complaints about how no one would do anything for her. Bitch and moan about everything and everyone; which finally turned to me. It was all my fault and I did not care and she wanted a divorce. But she also wanted to rape me financially. Surprise. This is properly the only time in history it has paid off to not have a pot to piss in. All she got was what I left behind. . . nothing!

But now I have everything. I have peace of mind. I have love from the heart and soul of another. I have a beautiful home in a tropical paradise called, Asingan, Pangasinan, Philippines. I have a dream as my wife and Best of all as my mother said, I AM ALIVE AGAIN.

Being alive again began in the form of one of the nightmares that would regularly come to haunt me with the demons living within my head. (A psychiatrist named Judith Golden told me in 1983 when I really started to fall apart, that I was building a mental wall trying to blank out the memories of Vietnam. What I had to do was dismantle this mental wall and simply let the demons within go. I could not. I was terrified of myself. In 1985 Dr. Judith Golden said there was nothing she could do for me if I could not look into myself.) Priny and I were asleep when one of these nightmares came to me. My struggling, tossing and turning, moaning and shouts into the night woke her up. Priny had no idea about Vietnam. She is a Filipina and Vietnam was never part of her life. She did not know what was going on inside my brain. She only knew that the man she was falling in love with was having a bad dream. She took me into her arms and whispered softly into my ear, ōItÆs alright.ö When she did this, I was still asleep, still living within my nightmare. But I heard her! I heard her soft oriental voice telling me that everything was alright. Just like that, ōSNAP!ö my mental wall was gone and with it, all the demons I was holding behind it. It was the first time anyone had spoken to me behind my mental wall. Behind that wall was the real me who had gone to Vietnam so many years ago. No one had talked to the real me for twenty seven years! I was back. But the last time anyone talked to the real me I was only twenty one years old. So here I am, feeling like a twenty one year old in the body of a fifty two year old man. My mother and father watched as I went through the digression of my life after Vietnam. My mother expresses her feelings to Priny for coming into my life and bringing me back to the light of the living by saying, ōPriny, you are the answer to my prays!ö

And so it is, my past can be remembered without having to relive it again, I can sleep at night without torment, my life now has reason and purpose, I am loved as I have never felt before and I love from a part within myself I never knew existed. I thank God for giving my life to Priny. She is my world, she is my life and best of all, she is my wife.

Rick Boutcher
