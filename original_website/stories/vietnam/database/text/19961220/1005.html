The Good part of Vietnam

I reflect upon my time as a combat paratrooper in Vietnam with the 1st Calvery of the U.S. Army (12/65-12/66) I mostly remember the good times. Some of the good times were the close friendship that was felt all around because only we were helping each other make it. Sure their were bad times; lost or badly wounded friends, constant fear, being crapped on when we came home, etc. But why think of that ? I think of - the beautiful countryside, the fun of flying in a helicopter, the good feelings knowing - people were being helped, both fellow soldiers and the South Vietnamese. No one could even begin to convince me that we did not do the right thing by trying to help.That is the main reason I started Los Angeles Vietnam Veterans Alliance (LAVVA) in 1990. That is - to keep the fact going - that Vietnam Veterans deserve constant R&R ( RESPECT AND RECOGNITION.)
