To anyone who might read: First I'd like to say "great job" to however put together this site. It's incredible. As you obviously know from my information, I was born well after the end of Vietnam. But the undenyable rage I felt when hearing how veterans from Vietnam were treated on their return flight made the War more interesting to me. First off, if you happen to be one of those who threw the dog excrement or spit on the soldiers, well, you have to live with yourself. You have to attempt to go to sleep at night knowing that you were the ones who did these things. I wish to God every time I think about the hell that these men went through and the treatment they recieved on their homecoming that somebody would have stuck you on the plane and crashed it square into the Pacific Ocean. America, in all it's problems, is still a good place. However, it will not remain a good place as long as you people still exist in this country. Let's just hope ignorance and cowardice is not hereditary.

Steve Watts
1979
