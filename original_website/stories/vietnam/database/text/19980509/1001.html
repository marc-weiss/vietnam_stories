I'm just an era Vet. I served 67-70 U.S. Army. It seems like a life time ago. God "We" were so young then, all of us. While stationed at Fort Dix, NJ; way back in 1970, I decided to go visit our nation's capitol. While passing in front of the While House a Hippy type boy dressed in bellbottoms and tie died shirt came up to me and spit at my shoes. He called me a baby killer. I told him that I havent been to Vietnam yet; he said it didnt matter, and spat on my shoes again. The next thing I knew some cops were pulling me away from him. I had beaten the crap out of him, and til this day I don't remember hitting him. The cops took my story, and sent me on my way. You have to understand, I was in my class B's. I guess that the boy didn't like the way I was dressed.

Take Care, Brothers

Welcome Home, Brothers of the Night
