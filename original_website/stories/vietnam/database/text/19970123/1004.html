Questions...without answers?

Why did my father want to return to Europe, where he fought in World War II?
Why don't I want to return to Vietnam?
Will the Gulf War veterans want to return to the site of "their" war?
Are we, the generations, different?
Are we different because of the war in which each of us fought?
Why did my friend have to die?
Why did I live?
Why do I feel guilty for surviving?
