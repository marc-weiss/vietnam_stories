The book, Vietnam: Our Story One on One was compiled by Gary Gullickson. Gary Gullickson surved with the U.S. Marine Corps. It has true stories of 59 Vietnam Veterans. This book covers growing up in high shcool, complete Vietnam experience, and breifly to present time. This book tells about Vietnam in the only way war can be described, with honesty and truth. Proceeds from this book have went back to help veterans and their families. 530 pages of the best and most honest acount of the Vietnam War.
