I WENT TO VIETNAM IN 1968. I SPENT A YEAR IN QUIN HNON. I VISITED SAIGON, LONG BHIN, AND LOTS OF OTHER FORGOTTEN NAMED TOWNS.{ I DROVE MY JEEP THROUGH THE VALLEY A FEW TIMES}. I REMEMBER LONG BHIN. AN ANT HILL OF MEN DUG INTO THE EARTH FOR PROTECTION FROM THE MOSQUITOES OF MODERN WARFARE ROCKETS, MORTARS, SNIPERS, SAPPERS, RIFLE, AND MACHINE GUN FIRE. HOLD-UP IN BUNKERS MADE OF SAND BAGS AND STEEL DRUMS. HOLD-UP WITH POT AND SMACK AND THE ROLLING STONES

I REMEMBER TRACERS IN THE NIGHT. LONG TONGUES OF DEADLY GREEN AND RED FIRE. I REMEMBER THE WHOOSH OF ROCKETS THE SCREAM OF ARTILLERY AND MORTAR ROUNDS. I REMEMBER THE WOUNDED IN THE HOSPITALS. THE WOUNDED FROM BOTH SIDES. ONE MISSING AN ARM, ONE MISSING A LEG, SOME MISSING BOTH. SOME LIVING IN TENTS , SOME IN BARRACKS...SO SURREAL.

I REMEMBER HOT NIGHTS, MOSQUITOES, RAIN FOR DAYS AND DAYS. AN OCCASIONAL CALL HOME FROM THE MARS STATION, WAR GAMES, A CUT ON THE HEAD, MY BIRTHDAY, BOBBY KENNEDY IS SHOT, A DAY TRIP "UP THE MOUNTAIN" WITH LUNCH AT THE HO JO'S OF THE FAR EAST. THE AMMO DUMP BLEW UP TWICE ...BOTH TIMES BY ACCIDENT. A SAPPER ATTACK-HELICOPTERS-ROCKETS-TRACERS-PEOPLE KILLED. MEN UNHAPPY, MEN WHO DIDN'T WANT TO FACE DEATH.

I REMEMBER PHRASES COINED BY MY PREDECESSORS, IN COUNTRY, SHORT TIMERS, LIFER, GOOK, SLOPE, WHITE MICE, MPC, P, IN COMING, R&R ,AND BACK IN THE WORLD. R&R MEANT TRIPS TO SIDNEY, HONOLULU, SINGAPORE, THAILAND. A DOZEN OTHER PLACES EQUALLY UNREAL. THE RETURN TO BATTLE AND DEATH AND BLOOD AND GORE; DREAMS OF HOME AND FAMILY;DREAMS OF FIRE AND DEATH. I DREAMED OF GOING HOME...BUT I COULDN'T GO HOME NOT BACK TO THE WORLD!

I QUIT SMOKING. NO ONE BELIEVED ME. THEY STILL WANTED TO BUM CIGARETTES. BUT I DID QUIT AND I STILL DON'T. IT HAS BEEN MORE THAN TWENTY-FIVE YEARS NOW. IT ALL SEEMS LIKE SUCH A LONG TIME AGO. I DON'T THINK MUCH ABOUT THE WAR NOW. NEVER DID ACUALLYT. NOT UNLESS I GO TO THE MONUMENT OR READ A NEWSPAPER CLIPPING OR A BOOK ABOUT VIETNAM OR SEE A FILM.

NONE OF THE FILMS ARE EVEN CLOSE TO WHAT I SAW. WHAT I DID. NONE OF THE BOOKS BEAR ANY RESEMBLANCE TO MY EXPERIENCE. I DIDN'T KILL ANYONE. I WASN'T SHOT AT. I DIDN'T STEP ON ANY MINES. I DIDN'T LOSE AN ARM OR LEG OR EYE. ALL I LOST WAS ONE YEAR TWO WEEKS AND A FEW HOURS OF 1968-
