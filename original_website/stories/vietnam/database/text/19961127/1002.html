My very best friend, who I met in the Navy in Hawaii in75 was a Force Recon Grunt from 67-68 in "Nam". He was at Khe Shan. He eventually wound up in the Navy after finishing a tour in theCorps. I loved this man like a brother, We kept in touch for many years, I partied with him all over the United States, as we could hook-up during duty stations. We drank alot, and smoked alot of alot of stuff, I cherished his great wife, and was Godfother to his only son. We parted ways in 1989, when I no longer could stand his my way-or the highway attitude. We were bothe cpo's in the Navy. I listened for many years about "the kills". The stress was alot for him to carry. He did his job well for the "recon" boys. He has slid into the alcoholic haze of self pity and long since lost his family. I miss him, will always miss him... He made me a man, raised me, showed me the ropes, and we had grande times! Oh did he know how to live. He lives within 45 minutes of my house, but I have no way of contacting him. Oh how I miss you, o mighty warrior. I always supported you and now miss you. Would like to see you and have a beer. Where are you John R. Stevenson? Your little Brother, Donnie Ackerman.
