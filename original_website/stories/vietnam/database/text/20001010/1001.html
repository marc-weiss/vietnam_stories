Friend or foe, in time and place...my mirrored imaged menace, it's time to walk our line.

"No", I've wrapped myself in a blue, white, and red blanket,... and kept warm from all those cold days.

But today I've shed my quilt and learned to live through yesterdays memories.

A broken sride, a shattered dream, a fallen friend, and a bittered nation. All have paid a visit and all have lost their ways.

A new day has risen, with a smile that could cast a winter night into spring morning by early light.

My new frind is "I", passed by day and day again. I've tried to forget and let it go.

Now, standing high above the fields, I can still see my brothers, and soon, they will see theirs.Only now I know I've fallen and risen once again.

I, alone, have passed this day by. To my brothers and Nation, only now can I cry.

Jude Scaglione
1977
Totowa, NJ, U.S.
