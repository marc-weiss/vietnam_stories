My dad is (was) a coward. Most of the men who were drafted went to Nam. Not my dad. He enlisted into the Marines and when he saw that he was going to Nam he shot his self in the foot. I live with this shame everyday. I beg for forgiveness from all the Vets for something my dad did so long ago. I feel that he should have went and fought along with the rest of you. If I had been born earlier and had the chance to fight by your sides I would have. The war not only affected the ones who were there but it also affected the ones who were too young. I went to the moving wall a few weeks ago and lingered at each name asking for forgiveness.

Richard Gore
1966
Houston, Texas USA
