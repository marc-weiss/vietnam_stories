CAN YOU IMAGINE BEING 18-20 YEARS OLD, FRESH FROM YOUR ADOLESENCE, WAKING UP ONE DAY AND FINDING THAT YOU WILL BE IN HELL FOR THE NEXT YEAR. THIS HAPPENED TO ME AND MOST OF THE 2,000,000 MEN WHO SERVED IN THE GOD-DAMNEST, MOST RIDICIOULIOS, INHUMANE WAR THERE WAS TO EVER BE. WE HAD NO, AND I AGAIN SAY NO REASON TO BE IN VIETNAM, EXCEPT TO MAKE THE ESTABLISHMENT RICH, RICHER AND RICHEST. TO EVERY SINGLE PERSON WHO HAD TO ENDURE THE INHUMANE TREATMENT OF THIS STUPID WAR, TO EVERY FAMILY WHO SUFFERED BECAUSE OF IT THEN AND NOW I WANT TO SAY THANK YOU. I AM NOT ANGRY AT THE DRAFT DODGERS, FOR I FEEL THERE JOB WAS ALSO HARD. I AM VERY ANGRY AT THE GOVERNMENT OF THAT TIME AND THE US CITZENS WHO SAID "GO, AND FIGHT SON/DAUGHTER, IT IS FOR THE BETTERMENT OF THE UNITED STATES. YOU WERE THE ENEMY. WHERE WERE THEY WHEN WE RETURNED WE GOT SHAFTED BY A VERY CORRUPT US GOVERNMENT AND EVEN MORE CORRUPT MORAL MAJORITY. I WILL NEVER FORGET OR FORGIVE THESE SICK BASTARDS.

THOMAS DINEEN
1948
