Mr. Greenberg

Tall, thin, rabbinically earnest- like Noam Chomsky - he - was wasted on us 6th graders. I remember one winter afternoon when he dramatically released the blinds to show us a red-gold-orange sun, "made more brilliant by atomic testing" he uttered bitterly. He even had us read John Hersey's Hiroshima 1958.

Ten years later the shit would hit the fan. I saw him briefly at Brooklyn College when we protested the firing of part-time lecturers who smoked dope, slept with students, spoke out against the war[s] in southeast Asia.

Before the pigs came, Greenberg slipped / slid away.

Ten years later the freaks metamorphosed into real estate developers. Greenberg was still plying his trade.
