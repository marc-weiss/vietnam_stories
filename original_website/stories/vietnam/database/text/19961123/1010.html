1970.@ The Induction Center

is BOMBED by Bill, who was 20, & other 19 year old "Hoosiers." Bill lost an eye in a mining accident, wore a patch which made him look severe except when he smiled His hands were rough, he was adept at demolitions:

"I blew it for Bobby who stepped on a booby- trapped 105 round, Quang Tri Province, Thursday, August 8,1969. When he died, he was short -'25 days and a wakeup'- that's what his bunk mate wrote us. "Bobby, man, Bobby..."

Bill lit a joint & passed it to me. I flipped on the car radio. No news about the Induction Center but 'this just in': Nixon's invasion of Cambodia & Laos provoked wide-spread strikes on college campuses today: "Let's hear it for the boys in the B-52s who got the job done.' Viet Nam was Kennedy, Johnson, Nixon, Ford's dry hump.
