the Commonwealth Club here in San Francisco. It was a profound talk
about the meaning for him of the war and the resistance and democracy.
He answered tough questions for about twenty minutes at the end. One
question was what about the veterans and what about the resisters. For
the veterans he said that what happened to them was unforgivable, that
they should have been treated as the sacrificial heroes that they were
and that it took too long to treat them as they deserved: with good
