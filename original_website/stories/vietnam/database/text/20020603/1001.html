I am obviously not old enough to have been a part of the Vietnam war, but I am an American so if affects my life. Ever scince I was little I have always said that I want to be somebody, but I thought then that being somebody ment being an actor or a singer, now to me being somebody means fighting for our country. I want to be a true American, I want to fight for freedom. The Vietnam War was what made me realize my true life meaning. I saw pictures and I read books and I talked to people. The men and women who fought in Vietnam were hero's. The war was truly hell on earth but they all fought through. They changed to course of life and for that I thank them everyday. Even though I have never met anyone who was in the war I still hold every single soilder in my heart with great pride and honor. I am a 19 year old female in the year 2002 who will fight for the rest of her life for the red, white, and blue.
