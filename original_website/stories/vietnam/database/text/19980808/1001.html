there i was, sitting in drawing class, when i heard the door slam loudly. all of us were stunned by the noise, all eyes were directed towards the door. there were two feet, with army boots-black- and baggy jeans. the jeans were faded a bit, but they were nice. Jncos, i suspected as the brand name. then there was the black Misfits t shirt. i looked up farther, and saw the boy... the new boy standing there, with his misfites t shirt and his baggy jeans. he was gorgeous, with his short curly brown hair, his blue eyes, his sexy smile. when he walked he leaned over with his hands in his pocket. ive never seen anyone walk like that before, so this was kind of interesting. i couldnt help but stare. he had his left ear pierced, with a god loop dangling through it. i wanted to touch him so bad, but i didnt dare try. he would think im a loon, or a nut. he would ask me why i was out of mendota. anyways, mr. fine started coming towards me. had he seen me staring, drooling? was he coming over to say he was offended? he sat right across from me at my table. i was guessing that was his new seat. he was making me very nervous. i felt awkward and dumb, sitting across from one of God's finest piece of work ever made. he tapped his long slender(and perfect)fingers on the table. i looked up, which was a mistake, because of those beautiful eyes of his caught mine. he smiled. "hey", he said. i wanted to melt right there all over the floor. " h..h...hi.", i tried to find the words, but it came out in a stutter. he looked at my artwork, and smiled. "very good.", he said. that was the last thing i needed to hear him say, i fell right there and then for him. i was in love. "thank you."

as the weeks went by, i learned to know his name. Griffin Everett. what a lovely name. we became good friends, inside of class that is. outside we would say our hello's and stuff, but that was about it. he was a sophmore and i was a freshman. one day he asked if i wanted to skip with him. he was a major skipper, and he was into marilyn manson and drugs, of course. he dated girls that would put out fast, which meant i would never go out with him. i said sure. he said it was our date.... we went outside, and he lit up a cigarette. "want one?", he asked. "i quit.", i lied. i really did smoke at that time, but he made me nervous so i didnt want to smoke infront of him. i dont know why, i just didnt feel comfortable. we talked and it got colder the more longer we were outside. "lets go get your coat.", he said. "no, im ok.", i said. i lied again. "well lets go back inside.", he smiled, concerned. "no. im ok." "would you at least wear my jacket?", he said taking it off. "sure." he put it on me. "you look really good in my coat.", he said. "thanks" after a few more minutes we went back into school and said our goodbyes, and headed into class. i went to drawing. " where were you?", my teacher said. i was 15 minutes late. " none of your business", i said. "melanie come here" "i dont want you hanging out with griffin. i kicked him out of here because hes a bad kid. you have more potential. stay away from him. his life is going nowhere."

but my teacher didnt know i knew a different side of griffin. griffin was good, deep down inside. i loved him so much. he was my bestfriend now.

one day we got in a fight because he said he would go out with me but he didnt want to hurt me because all of his other girlfriends he just uses for sex and money for drugs. i couldnt understand why even though he was being very sinsere of my feelings. i wrote him notes saying liar on them over and over.

now, it was may. it was the friday, the day of the dance. i had asked griffin to the spring dance. he said he already had plans to go to a party. i said it was ok. i got on my bus- i was going home til the dance started- and griffin came running and tapped on the window. i opened it. "no- i cant take this much from you", he handed it back. i pushed his hand back "keep it" "i couldnt-" "please!", i said. he looked at me. "ill pay you back." "no, dont worry about it." "thank you melanie!", he said, and the bus pulled away. that was the last time i ever saw griffin, my bestfriend, again.

on the night of the dance, griffin used my money to get drugs. his father found the drugs, and turned him into the police. they arrested him, and moved him down to greenbay, wisconsin to rehab.i havent seen him since. now i am going to be a junior in highschool. griffin if your still out there i want you to know i love you dearly.and ive missed you so much.i will never have a bestfriend like you again. i blame myself for the night of the dance. if i hadnt given you any money- you wouldnt be gone. i love you and i want you to come home.
