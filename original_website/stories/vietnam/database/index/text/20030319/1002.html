this is a second posting for me today. after i read some of the articles i will state my feelings about being there.

i was an interpreter and served on several operations, having the opportunity to talk with people. the thing that impressed me most was their gentleness and also the statements made by most, "if you americans were not here our life would go on as it has for a 1000 years."

i ate well in some of their homes and still cook the food. my kids love it. i try to converse with other viets in the usa but my skills are shot. they enjoy talking with me and i to them.
