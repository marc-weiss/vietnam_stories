More than 30 years ago this country sent it's soldoers to a place 10,000 miles from thier homes with no more than their own fears. I am an 18 year old high school senior and a Private First-class in the National Guard. Part of my duty in the guard is to participate in local cerimonies and events. A large part of my participation is my depicion of a Vietnam era soldier. I talk to history goverment classes in local schools to try to educate young people about the sacrafices of our fathers and thier generation. This past memorial day I was involved in the cerimony and the reaction from the veterens of that era was amazing some laughed remembering fond memories some cried remembering lost friends but I'm just glad that I could have a part in honoring all the men who faught and died so many miles from home so many years ago.

PFC Jakob D. Honn
1980
Mt. Vernon, Il USA
