Eve: live-giver; the first woman

She was a gift given by God to man. She was created so that man would not be alone. He needed a helper to walk along side of him. He needed a companion to share spiritual, emotional, and sexual pleasures. She was the first bride. A woman is to leave her father and mother and cleave unto her husband. She was naked spiritually, emotionally, and physically in front of her husband. They were not ashamed nor did they keep secrets from one another.

Until, she met the serpent. He tricked her with his cunning words. She was deceived and accepted the fruit that would make her wise, but this was not a wise decision. Sin and death entered into the world.

Her punishment was her husband ruling over her and delivering children with pain. She would live with a natural desire to overstep her husband. She would try to fight him for rulership. Yet, God restored her by clothing her with garment and giving her children.

Gina Renee Johnson
