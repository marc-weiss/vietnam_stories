The first time i laid eyes on Raymond Ellis was in basic training at the Naval Training Center in San Diego,Ca.Me, being from the cottonfields of Ark and him from copper pits of Bisbee Az, we definitely had nothing in common.After basic we both ended up in Danang VN.There we would share family and friends from our homes in the states.In Viet Nam ,like a lot of the other young men there,mostly 18 and 19 years old we would grow up fast and become very close friends.After the tour of duty Ray and i both ended up on the USS Orion out of Norfolk Va.When i got married, Ray was my best man and 6 where he met the brides maid and cousin of my wife.Then 6 months later he would take her as his wife.We have remained friends forever.We both have raised our own familys now and still happily married to the same two gals.Ray is back in AZ and i am still here in Ark.We still stay in touch, after all his wife has family here,so he has to come and see me.

Larry Hillis
1947
Forrest City,Ar, USA
