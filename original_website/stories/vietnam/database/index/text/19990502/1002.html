There are several stories that I could report here, but I will only give one here. I know that one incident that really made me stop and think happened in the back of an open duce and a half enroute to Siagon from FSB Mortain. You all know about the fine roads we had over there. Well just as we wewrepassing a rubber plantation and I was getting a pack of smokes from my pocket, we hit a large pot hole and I dropped them. I leaned over to pick my smokes up I heard the unmistakeable sound of an AK47 fire three quick rounds. Needless to say I immediatley assumed the prone position. After we got down the road a piece I got to m7y kneesand looked at the spot I had been sitting and there were holes in the slats of the truck right where my chest would have been. Now when I have to go to the Doctor and he tells me to quit smoking I tell them this little story. Ever so often though I stop and think what are the odds of a pack of ciggaretts let me survive my tour in Nam when so many others never came home. I really should stop writing now, but I want to add this: I wouldn'tgladly go back to Nam to fight another war ,but if my country asked me to go I would.
