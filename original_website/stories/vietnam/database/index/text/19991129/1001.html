INTROUDUCTION.

THIS STORY TELLS HOW A COMMON MAN IN TROUBLE.

I WAS IN MY BATHROOM WHEN THE BELL RUNG A MAN WHO WAS RINGING THE BELL SEEMS TO BE IN HURRY CAZ HE WAS CONTINUALLY RINGING THE BELL. I ABRUPTLY DECIDED NOT TO OPEN THE DOOR CAZ I WAS AFRAID OF. I THOUGHT MAY BE ROBBERS HAD COME OR MAY BE A DACATE HAD COME. SUDDENLY I FELT THAT HE HAD RUN AWAY FROM MY DOOR THIS THING FRIGHTEN ME SO MUCH. I THOUGHT NOW HE WAS GOING TO BREAK THE DOOR.

ABOUT ME I M LIVING IN NEWYORK NOT IN MAIN CITY. ACTULLY I M LIVING NEAR NEWYORK IN A TOWN AND I HAVE MY HOME FAR FROM THE USUAL DWELLERS. MY NAME IS BARNY CORNER HUNS AND I M ORPHAN MY AGE IS ONLY 32 I M NOT MARRIED YET. I M LIVING ALONE AND I HAVE MY OWN SHOP IN THE TOWN. I HAVE VERY GOOD NATURE I LOVE THE NATURAL BEAUTY U MAY SAY I M IN THOSE KIND OF PEOPLE THEY WANT TO LIVE IN PEACE WITHOUT INTRRUPTION OF ANYONE. I WAS TELLING U ABOUT THE MAN OR MEN WHO WAS RINGING THE BELL HE DID NOT COM BACK AFTER MID NIGHT PASSED I DECIDED TO OPEN THE DOOR JUST TO SEE WHAT HAD DONE. I OPEN THE DOOR AKIMBO AND TIRED TO INVESTIGATE THE SITUATION WITHOUT OPEN THE DOOR FULLY SUDDENLY I SAW A THING AND STUNND TO SAW

TO BE CONTINUED

M.B.A SHAKIR
1979
