No story, but a question.

January 25, 1968, the USS Pueblo was captured by the North Koreans. The next day My Guard unit and 3 others were activated called to federal service). My unit was the first National Guard unit to enter a war zone since WWII. We were sent to Cannon AFB, Clovis, New Mexico for training, and on April 30th we landed at PhanRang AFB,RVN. Now the question: Will you please do a story on the 120th Tactical Fighter Squadron from the Colorado Air National Guard. Keep in mind that of the 355 of us were sent to Viet Nam with the 120th, but ALL of the maintenance troops were, upon arriving in VN) transferred to the 35th Field Maintenance Squadron. We were taken out of our home unit to fit the USAF installation at Phan Rang.

Will I get a reply?
