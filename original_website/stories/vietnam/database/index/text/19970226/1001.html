H. takes the top off from the doctors head but his brain is still sitting there like an ice cream cone when i pull him from betweeen the rocks he moans his brain falls out and rice pours from his pack into where his brain used to be while our lady is getting a close shave from one of my native american squad members im pissing on the doctors brain to remove weeds and dirt plus to show him my appreciation for having tryed to kill me and my buddies . this is not made up its not a brag its just alittle part of what happened on a very lucky day in my life if you want to call it luck.
