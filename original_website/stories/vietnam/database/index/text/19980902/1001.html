I'm not a vet, and I have no relitives that were in Vietnam. But my grandfather was in Korea. To this day he will not talk about it. He recived the Soldiers Metal for rescuing a man fron a mine field. Thats all I know about what happened to him in Korea. He will not talk about it. To ask a warrior to dig out the horrors and fears that transpired in their personal history is like asking a man to dig out his heart and eat it. I have studied and read everything I could find on the Vietnam War. I have one thing to say about these men. They are of the most heroic men in history. The phsycological effects of fighting an army of gurilla warriors where there are no rules, no Geniva Convension is devistating. You cannot tell when the enemy will strike. Then if we did get in the position to give them something to think about, they would just hop on the other side of the border. The polititions in Washington said not to presue them across the border. I think that would be very bad for moral. We never got to show them what it would be like if our generals were running the war insted of the president. I think we would have had a better chance of our troops doing some real damage insted of being sent to die. But that does not change my view of sending troops to help our allies. I belive we did a good thing trying to stop the spread of Communism. It showed that the U.S. would not stand for Communism to spread its wings without a fight. When I join the Marines I will do whatever my Commander and Cheif asks of me. If it means fighting a loosing battle and dieing, so be it. The people who protested and burned the draft cards and picketed and refused to do what their country asked of them were cowards. If you are one of the individuals that sat around smoking pot while our men died, I pitty you. For you will always know you let your country down. You will always know that when Uncle Sam needed you, you spit in his face. I have nothing more to say about that subject because it breaks my heart to know my country fights to keep these people alive and free. I wish to ask everyone a question, do you belive President Kenedy was a good president? If you do, you should know it was partly because of our beloved president that there was a war. He said the U.S. would try to stop communism. We did just that. For those of you veterins that feel like no one thinks you did the right thing by fighting, you can always know that I stand by you and this country, and I am willing to pay for it with my life if need be.
