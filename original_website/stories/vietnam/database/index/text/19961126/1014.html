The questions is how am I different today because of Viet Nam? I guess the question should be how would I have been if I hadn't gone? My value structure came from a family structure that was very patriotic. In other words (not so politically correct) it is the way that American males repay our country for the freedom that we all enjoy. That is enlisting into the military to repay our country for our liberties. This has affected my politics because I am now more aware. My value system was based on survival skills that have more or less matured into a set of coping skills. Relationships were no existent. I was 20 yrs. old when I got back. Who wanted to date a 20 yr. old killer? It took a long time to find a love that I have today. Proud to say I've been married to the same woman for 26 years. God bless her!
