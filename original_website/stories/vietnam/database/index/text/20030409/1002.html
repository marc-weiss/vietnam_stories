From late 1967 until the Spring of 1970, I was a Marine radio operator. My parent unit was Comm Company, 7th Comm Bn. We got assigned all over Eye Corps to augment the grunts.

The technicolor movie never goes off.

The meaning it shares with me daily is that every day is extra.

Terry Gabriel
1946
