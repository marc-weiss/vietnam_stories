I am looking for Vietnam vets with a connection to Brooklyn, NY--either that are from Brooklyn, or have some connection to it-- for a photography project. For the project, we are photographing things soldiers brought back from Vietnam: the mementoes that they kept from the war (uniforms, too), or the photos, letters, and other things that they carried with them during their service. If you have any such mementoes or saved items please contact me at JTOLSSON@aol.com. This is for a project that will eventually be a book--kind of a visual version of Tim O'Brian's great book "The Things They Carried." Any help you can give would be greatly appreciated.

JT Olsson
