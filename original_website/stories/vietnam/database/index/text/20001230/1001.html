It was 19:30 hrs on the 14 June 1968 when I made my first kill in Nam. The platoon was in good moral, There was only the destinctive sound of AK47 to break the silence.He lifted his head from beneath the undergrowth, I raised my rifle and fired two rounds off, the first one hit his chest and sent him crashing to his knees, the second round cought him in the side of the head, This is when I knew the transformation from boy to man had occured.

Hank Dodge
1940
Alabama
