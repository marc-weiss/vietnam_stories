like many of my generation, i spent the war protesting- sitting in and picketing. how did the war effect my life- it's hard to say. i was cynical about the american government to begin with. the war certainly increased this tendency. at one point i seriously considered taking up arms against the government. the longer tha war continued, the angrier i got. i was very upset with my roommate at college who decided to go vietnam.

the changes the war made to my thinking was mostly to intensified my feelings and outlook and pushed me towards a more extremist position.

the most vivid memory is of sitting in at the vietnamese mission to the un, being arrested and taken to jail.

today i continue to be cynical and angry about the roll of the us government in the world.

larry hertz
1944
albuquerque,nm usa
