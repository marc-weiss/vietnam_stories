This was a war that brought us all grey values. The differences between right and wrong, good and bad, all were blurred. And we who were born into this generation were denied, in our liberation of the 1960s and 1970s, the understanding of values. The movement of my youth was born of distrust, anger, revolution, and radicalism. How it came to be clothed in the bright colors of love and flower power, I'll never understand. Maybe it was the effects of acid.
