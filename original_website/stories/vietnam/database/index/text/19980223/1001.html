my story began quite some time after my tour of duty in vietnam in 1968.i had forgotten about the time i had served in vietnam,the friends made and lost.when the book dear america-letters home from vietnam,came out iread through the book and found 2 letters written by dennis lane and mike ransome.dennis lane was in my squad mike ransome was our plt/leader.iwas present for both of these mens deaths.dennis "rocky" as we called him was hit by shrapnel may 1968.south china sea area died from head wounds.my self and others put him on a chopper.mike ransome died of complications,after he and members of2nd squad set off a mine.3rd squad came to their aid,again i helped put him on a chopper.reading these letters it hit home to me,these 2men were writing to their family.how they must miss them,Imiss then to.j.k.11/brigade 4/3.3rd plt.americal division

john kelly
1948
