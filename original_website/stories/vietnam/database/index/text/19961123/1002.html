Hanoi, Jane?

"Some of the best people in America today are behind bars." -Jane Fonda, 1968

like Hera, Zeus's consort, she's everywhere: on videos, cassettes, CDs. At ball games, parades, doing the Tomahawk Chop & the Persian Gulf Rag. living high on the hog in Georgia, knows for its juicy peaches & electric chair, "affectionately" called Yellow Mama. Still she pisses people off. Beautiful women should be spread, not heard, according to Jimmy Swaggart & a recent Gallup Poll conducted by CNN news & the Heritage Foundation. She is annoying. Hollywood High, followed by Finishing School. Her father's good looks, her silicone boobs, her unbearably shrill voice, in spite of elocution lessons.
