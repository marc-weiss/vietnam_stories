I was from the 34st airbourne and i was a private at the start when me, the sarge and my platoon were patrolling the Da Nang when Charlie came from no where and wiped out half the platoon and most of my legs. The Hueys came and wiped those bastards out. I have never walked since but was proud to serve this shithouse country of ours.

Nathan Harding
1943
New York

34st airbourne, Da Nang
