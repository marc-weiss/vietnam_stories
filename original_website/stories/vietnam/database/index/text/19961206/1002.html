I look upon this black stone wall and read the names of men once tall I hear the widows, mothers, brothers cry and ask the age old question.....Why?

They gave thier lives for cause unsure never knowing why they must endure that green and sweaty earthly hell before a final tolling of the bell

These were my brothers one and all brave hearts whom destiny bade fall I pray that we shall someday know why they were robbed of chance to grow

Terry Martin
1939
Middleton, Idaho
