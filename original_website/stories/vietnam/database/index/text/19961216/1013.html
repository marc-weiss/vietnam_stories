I was born in 1960 so I was a child and I was confused about the war. I got a POW bracelet, a bunch of my friends got them but I don’t think we really knew the implications back whole then of what those were and I learned later on in high school and college more about what that meant.

I just wanted to share a memory, the first time I saw the wall was sometime in the mid- eighties and I was there alone and I watched a family go over and look up a loved one and I watched them go find that person on the wall and go and touch it and start to cry. And I will always, always remember that. It moved me immensely. And I am probably a different person because of that...

I think the memorial is wonderful and too bad there is so much controversy around that as there was around the war itself.

Julie Goldman
1960
Boston, MA
