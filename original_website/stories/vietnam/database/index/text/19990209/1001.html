Submitted by Arthur Cook

Acting Commander on 21 Aug 1968 USA I am proud of the all men who fought on 21 Aug 1968 in the 1/5 (M) Inf. They fought well for their country. I only found out about this award recently. It has added closure to a mystery for me that has gone on for 30 years. What SSGT Young did on that day was truly heroic. He allowed the rest of us to reach safety after we were so hopelessly outnumbered. I'll remember him always in my heart. Thanks to his heroic efforts I and my fellow soldiers in the unit are able to be home with their families. My heartfelt thanks SSGT Marvin Young and to his family. Tuesday, December 29, 1998
