I joined the marines out of high school I was stationed in Penelton in16 area 11th Marines I wanted to go home on leave but instead I got orders for Nam I left the next morning for Okinawa this was in 65 After about 4 months on the Island We arrived in DaNang . I was transferred to the 12th Marine Motor pool. I got to participate in Harvestmoon. Then Was transferred to 8inch and went to Phu Bai. While there i was in Operation Hastings. All this time I never got to see a Cong I lost my Co Commander to a landmine was sniped at never the less sit stilll scared the tar out of me. My seconed tour was the scariest I was in a Cap Program I was in a little vill called Nin Bind. Southwest of An Wa Was told a bn o nva was moving to our sector and to move to a more secure area. Arrived in another Vill outside Hoi Ann. we moved to a new area with no cover except the sky This was during tet in 68. Units were hit all around us I was terrifyed sweating and freezing at the same time. Then this PF said to me not to worry the vc did not want me or my unit I do not know why but I am here to tell about it. I was close to rotation so i asked the first sgt if i could come to the hq and work as a clerk. some rookie was dying to go to the bush so he took my place. he was killed two weeks later. It was quite an experience . I still belive in my country. I met some good Marines and some bad ones but that is life. I tried not to get to close I did not want to loose a friend. I do not have any particular political party I vote for the person and what they stand for. I belive that you only go around once so enjoy each day to the fulliest.

gary l wright
1945
Kewanee il
