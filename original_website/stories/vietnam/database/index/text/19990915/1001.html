Vietnam. I wish it had never happened. I was a sucker to believe my elders. But what the hell? Now, I have a disability. I can just sit around with my fellow Vietnam Veterans at the local VA Hospital and complain about how bad our lives have been and will be. The funny thing about having a PTSD disabilty is that everybody thinks your a living on easy street. Yeah, I get money every month from the Government. It's like I planned to do this back in high school. I went to the guidance counselor and told him that I hope a good war comes along. Then I will join the Army, go off to some exotic land and see a whole bunch of crap, come home, live like a dog for 20 years and then live off the VA the rest of my days. The crazy thing is that maybe I am better off today. 10 years ago I was homesless. Five years ago I was living in an Old Soldiers Home. And today, Well, I am married, have a home in the suburbs, a car, credit card debt. Of course, nobody gives a rat's ass if I went to Vietnam or not because it really doesn't mean anything anymore as much as we want it to. I just want to be left alone to live in peace. Screw Vietnam and everything associated with it. Screw the military mind set of this nation. and Screw me for being such a putz.

Tim
48
Richfield, Mn. USA
