I served with the 5th SFGA., Macsog, CCN. Aug 68 thru Jul 71. Vietnam changed my life. because I cannot even think of what my live would be like without Vietnam. The fact that the Vietnam war did happen. I feel that it changed every person born after the war started. I survived the war and survived the life after the war made a change, My Values of life. is different compared to other people who had no contact with the war. I take chances in life that most other people do not. My sex life changed because of the war I became a man who cannot be with only one women. Must have more throughout my life. I lost many friends in Vietnam. Since then I only care for vets and men who served. I retired SF 10 years ago. Mastersergeant with 23 years service. Vietnam is with me every day of my life. I still remember the war as if it was yesterday. I cannot forget the war. even if I wanted to. I was wounded several times and, the Nurse corps in Vietnam I owe my life. I remember a Nurse named Kathy. Who took care of me. Danang. 1968 She was a calm women. I watched her put a plastic bag over 8 men who came to the Hosp with me. I was the only one who was alive. she cryed and I noticed that she took every death personnal. her face and Mem is with me forever. I thank her and all like her. Yes Vietnam changed my life. I will say this all that did not come home will live in my heart as long as I live. Thank you

Kenneth Bird
1947
Tacoma, Wa. USA
