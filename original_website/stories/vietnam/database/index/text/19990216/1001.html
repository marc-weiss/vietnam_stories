As a little girl, I never understood why my daddy woke up screaming and crying for his life. Not long ago, my mother told me of the horrors he saw and the pain he lived. He would go to sleep everynight not knowing if he would live to see tomorrow. He still goes to bed that way because he can't let go. To this day, I cannot, nor will I ever know the fear he felt. I can't even comprehend that he was only the age of eldest brother - 19. He fought for his life in the war. Now he fights for his life in his own war everyday.

"Patience"
1983
Cincinnati, Ohio
America
