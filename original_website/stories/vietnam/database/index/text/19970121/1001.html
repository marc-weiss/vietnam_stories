This site has re-awaken long dormant feelings, thoughts, dreams, of a lifetime long ago. From a patriotic 18 year old who went down to the draft board on his birthday to register, to a full on long hair dope smoking pinko draft card burining protestor, Nam and its wake changed me and my generation for ever. Following on the JFK killing, the deaths of Robert Kennedy and ML King, then the utter bullshit of Viet Nam, my generation became for ever exiled from mainstream american politics and beliefs.

While we went on to build careers, from Jane Fonda marrying wealth to Tom Hayden's wanting to be mayor of L.A., most of us just got involved in day to day living. But the seed remained in our lives. We smoked doped quietly, we voted mildly, we worked and we paid our taxes, and we knew that Authority was to be avoided. I.E. something left over from the draft.

I became a psychologist directly in relation to the experiences and feelings I had and survived from those days. This site awakes feelings most joyfull and dreadfull.

How did this affect me, you ask, as if you have just given me a new skinner box, and I am some white rat ready to be studied????

Sorry but there, the site and I part company. Too many questions being asked, with too many eyes reading the answers.
