It was sometime 1969, QuangTri combat base. I was in a tower watching helicopters land. I noticed a particular copter had come down rather hard and wondered why. I was close enough that I could see that blood was running out of the copter. The next day I was talking with one of the corpsman and he told me that that particular copter had come in with 6 dead Marines in the back as well as the copilot and that the pilot had died just after landing. I don't know who that pilot was but surely there should have been a CMH for him in that he got all those guys back before dying. It seems as though it happened yesterday. -- Glenn N. Wallin EX-Navy Seabee (MCB74) Wally's Workshop All Around Nice Guy
