Wild Bill Dardenne Missouri 1947

Keywords: Where Ever You Go, There You Are!

Story:
I started out trying to fulfill my parents wishes. They wanted me to become a physician, I wanted to be a musician/actor. I volunteered for the draft in 1966 because my best friend was drafted. The ARMY transformed me into a combat medic and my friend into a pointman. Bob survived and so did I. I never became a doctor, I did finish college. I have worn many hats since the 60's. Teacher, musician, broadcaster, corporate vp and now a counselor/teacher. What I still need to fulfill is my acting/creative goal. No matter what happens to you on the road of life, there really are no dead ends. You just have to build your own road sometimes, and all roads connect and lead home to your inner peace. It is that peace which is the foundation of our very being. Peace to all, and thank you for serving God and country on this Veterans Day.

Wild Bill
