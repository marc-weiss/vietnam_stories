An announcement from your "hosts" at P.O.V.
(3-10-98)

We are proud to announce that the dialogues which began on these pages in November 1996 are continuing on the Vietnam Veterans Memorial Fund site.

Although it will no longer be possible to post new messages or create new threads on this bulletin board, all messages posted to date will continue to be available on a "read-only basis."

We'd like to thank everyone who has made the discussions here a true "dialogue across differences." As some of the "farewell" messages on topic 227 say, many lives have been changed for the better.
