What's Love Got to Do With It?
Lisa talks about having a relationship and being in love

Lisa:
The most thing you need in a relationship is like trust. Like see, I've been going out with Willie ... since I was 12 ... up until now. I'm 16, and I didn't go out with anybody else, and I was just totally dead in love with him. I didn't care about anybody else.

He used to cheat on me all the time and then I couldn't trust him anymore. You have to be in love and be happy. You know? I was in love, but I wasn't happy. Like, before, he was like, "I don't want you to go here, I don't wan't you to go there. I don't like this friend, don't stay with her. "

And its just not right, you know? Who [is he] to tell me what to do? I mean, [he's] not my father. I don't listen to my father, why should I listen to [him]? You know? Who [is he]?
