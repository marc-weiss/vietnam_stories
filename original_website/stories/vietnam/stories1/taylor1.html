In 1963 I toured S-E Asia on my own after one and a half years in South Korea with the D.O.A. as a Recreation Specialist. The U.S. 'Military Advisors' in South Vietnam numbered 13,000 at that time. The people in all of S-E Asia thirty five years ago were the poorest I had ever seen. The escalation of the war in Vietnam never seemed justifiable.

Carol Taylor
