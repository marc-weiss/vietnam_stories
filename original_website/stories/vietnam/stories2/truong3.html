The real heroes are those parents who gave up their freedom, lifestyle and country to provide their children with a wonderful future. I find it ignorant that film-makers glorify the war but forget about the other heroes...the normal Vietnamese family who either stayed or left to a foreign land and have now made a comfortable life for their children at the sacrifice of their own.

We should stop debating on whether the War was right or wrong but focus on the wonders of such strong human beings such as my mum and dad. Most American soldiers were able to go home...we had to escape or remain in the War zone. Please do not forget.
