Larry T. Fryske

TEMPERATURES:

I arrived In-country at Cam Rahn Bay on 15 March 1969 it was about 90 degrees of breath-arresting heat. Thirty years later, when I touched THE WALL it was cold as ICE, Temperature 10 below, a flash of cold shoots up my arm to my shoulder. But when I walked out and up away from the wall I felt a COOL BREEZE, Temperature: CLEANSING...
