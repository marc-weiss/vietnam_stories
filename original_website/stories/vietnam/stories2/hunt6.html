I didn't live in a foxhole, I didn't do patrols, and I saw and did a lot that in my later years I wouldn't do again. Nam Jan 67-Feb 68 I came home. I called my dad in California and asked him not to tell anyone but I was on my way home. He met me at the Detroit airport alone. My sisters were mad because they had signs and all. That was why I wanted just dad there. He knew why. He was a Marine in WWII.

Yeah I remember a lot....and more. you just got two hours of my peckin'. Later
