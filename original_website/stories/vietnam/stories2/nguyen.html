Ha Nguyen

I left Vietnam when I was 3 years old. Now at 19 and in college, I've learned to live the American way. I do not know much about my country; only what my parents and grandparents had told me. The sweet memories of the past, a home that they long for but sacrificed in order to come to America to insure a better life for their children.

Most of my family reside here in the States. My mom's side of the family are still back in Saigon. My mom would tell me how much I have going for me. She only has my dad, my brothers and I. She had left everything and everyone she had grew up with for me. She is there at a touch of a phone call to comfort me and yet she really doesn't have anyone.
