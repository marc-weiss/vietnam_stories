The most memorable experience -- I remember we would sit on Sunday evening in the Student Union and watch television and I think it was there when we were all sitting around watching the Smothers Brothers on Sunday night and Nixon came on and talked about, with these charts, bombing Cambodia. I think this was in 1970, because that is what led to the protests at Kent State, which then led to the shootings at Kent State.

David Hurwitz (edited excerpts from "story circle" of anti-war activists convened in Dayton, Ohio at the request of P.O.V.)
