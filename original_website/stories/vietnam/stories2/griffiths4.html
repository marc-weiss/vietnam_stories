Ann Mills Griffiths

I have seen POW/MIA families' faith in our government shattered by political inattention, deceit, distortions and incompetence. I have also seen their faith renewed by the seriousness and dedication of President Ronald Reagan and others. Conspiracy and cover-up theories are a myth; there are too many good people involved. Besides, the U.S. Government is totally incapable of an EFFECTIVE cover-up.
