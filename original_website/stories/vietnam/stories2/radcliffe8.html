I think the country has learned a lot from our sacrifice and our experiences and I think we are going to be a constant reminder that we are not going to let anything like that happen again. I think that is one of the legacies of that war; that I don't think the country will ever let that happen again, to treat the veterans that way. They may not like the war, but hopefully they've learned and that's the reason I'm so involved in the historical aspects so that people will remember and not let the situation happen again.

Steve Radcliffe (edited excerpts from "story circle" of Vietnam Veterans convened in Dayton, Ohio at the request of P.O.V.)
