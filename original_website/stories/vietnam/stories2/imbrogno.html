Douglas Imbrogno

At first, you think -- what a dumb idea. Take one magnificent monument -- Maya Lin's Vietnam Veterans's Memorial on the Mall in Washington, D.C. Copy all 57,661 names on the polished black granite wall onto metal panels. Shrink the whole thing down to one-third the size of the original. Stack the panels in a truck, with a load of screwdrivers and struts. Ship the thing off on tour.
