I am writing a book from my perspective during that year. Our first child was born, my husband was wounded and transformed into a totally different man than I had married. When he returned, we buried the experience to move forward with our lives, not knowing that what we should have done was deal with the hurt and the wounds first.

It has been the "Mistress" in our relationship for the past 26 years and we are finally dealing with the pain - and making progress. I wonder why there has been little written from the wife's perspective? I fought my own war at home. I felt pride in surviving only to realize that I really didn't survive, only pretended I had.

This website will help many. I will check it regularly.

L. Hansmann, FROM THE WIFE'S POINT OF VIEW posted to dialog area 11/18/96 under "Comments on this Site"
