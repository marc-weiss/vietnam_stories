Renny Christopher

Now when I go to the Vietnam Veterans Memorial in Washington, or the California Vietnam Veterans Memorial in Sacramento, in my mind's eye I see the memorials in Viet Nam, as well. I have pictures of them on my desk, taken by David Berman. There is one in every town, a vertical stone rising above the thatch and tile roofs of the houses, inscribed "To Quoc Ghi Cung Cac Liet Si" ("the fatherland remembers the sacrifice of each fallen soldier").
