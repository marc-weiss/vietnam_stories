Mark Gaines

I served in the military during Vietnam and almost got orders for the place. When I got out what we were subject to was unreal. I was called a "baby killer" by a white middle class college student in Boston just because I happened to be on duty in uniform under direct orders to go carry out Coast Guard business. I was a part time EMT on the side so I didn't like that at all. I was training hard to save lives! That was just the beginning of hurts.
