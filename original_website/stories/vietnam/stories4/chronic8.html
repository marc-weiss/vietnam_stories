Mark Gaines

I have never seen America own up to anything about Vietnam. Not that it would help now, why bother. The Gulf War Veterans got their well deserved welcome back and I am glad they did.

I see that the Vietnam War destroyed the sense of common values and shared traditions. It tore the country apart. Since the issue was never addressed, it only got worse. Could this perhaps be a part of the drugs, illicit sex and shattered families that so divide America today?
