Horace Coleman

Down South, not far from The Redneck Riviera, while in a military "tech school" I went out with some of the guys (white) in my class to have a drink. I must have forgotten what country I was already in. We picked a restaurant and sat down. A Scottish waitress, still on her real green card, said to our table, "I'm sorry but I can't serve the other gentleman." I was "the other gentleman."
