PLEIKU RVN ALL 1968-1969

Forgive yourself for the things you did and the things you didn't do. It may be harder to realize that we we're not all born heroes; maybe we learn about courage from our failures and we become stronger as time goes by.

I thought I had the courage to toss a grenade and didn't. Maybe I was too scared, maybe it wasn't meant to be, maybe my hesitation caused my ambush patrol brother to get wounded. I didn't know the consequences of my decison and I am sorry. I forgive myself and choose to live a good life for those that gave their lives for us all.

Rick Horrigan
Submitted to the Dialog area Postwar trauma: Kicking it Nov. 27th,1996
