Mark Gaines

A few months later I got out. There were no jobs for me in what I knew how to do. All I could get was security guard minimum wage work. Eventually I did go back to school and retrain, but it messed up my jobs for a long time. The people who had not been in the military had all the good jobs it seemed. There was no recognition of the veterans at all; it seemed a lot of people would prefer we just went away.
