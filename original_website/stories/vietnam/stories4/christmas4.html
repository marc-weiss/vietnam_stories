Reed Van Billiard

I have a memory of a Christmas service in the field somewhere in the Delta. We were in a bad AO with a lot of contact, booby traps, and casualties. We secured an LZ for a visiting priest, and after landing he had to walk 100 meters through some elephant grass to our location. After the service the Priest continued on and more troops flew in to support us. We radioed them and advised the new arrivals to follow the path the priest used since it was safe, and to our dismay I watched booby traps explode where a priest had walked less than an hour before.
