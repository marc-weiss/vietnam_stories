I wanted my return trip to be a positive experience.
and I planned to share what I learned with my community.
But another major reason for my going
was the selfish desire to put some personal ghosts to rest.

I've been too busy since I got back
to examine whether or not I've gained any valuable new perspectives.
But last night, for the first time ever, I had a dream about Vietnam at peace.

Larry Rottman is the author of Voices From the Ho Chi Minh Trail
