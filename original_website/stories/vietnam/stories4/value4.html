I DIDN'T GO, CAN'T UNDERSTAND.

My wife and I just returned from a year-long bicycle trip around America. We saw so many destroyed Vietnam vets. You won't see them here, because they are just hanging on day to day. They don't even know what the www or email is. Mostly we saw them getting drunk or walking desert highways talking to themselves.

I was 4-F (bleeding ulcer)...I would have gone, I would have killed, and I might have been one of them (you). I didn't, and I'm not. I want to understand, but know I never will. I am writing a book about our travels, and doing a chapter on the vets we met. I need to hear from any of you who want to talk, Bob

Bob Rogers
Submitted to the Dialog Area, Beyond Vietnam, Dec. 11th, 1996
