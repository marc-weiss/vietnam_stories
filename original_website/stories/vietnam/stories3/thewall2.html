Dennis Sherwood

There were mementos: flowers, old photos, a toy truck, a baseball, shrines to remembering. Between two slabs a note was jammed in the crack, "Bob, I bring you a message from Sandy. You'll always be her love."

Another, with a photo, "Who would have known that I would grow up to look like someone I never even knew?"

And nearby, "Damn you brother, for not coming back."
