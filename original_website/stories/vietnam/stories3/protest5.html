I think that the time I and others spent fighting (protesting) against the war helped in some way to shorten the war. To me this means that I helped to save lives, and the time, effort and disruption to me was well worth it. I am proud of what I did and understanding of those who fought. It was not clear then what was right and what was wrong. I have come to believe that people find their own paths in life and I cannot accuse those who chose to fight.

Jed Proujansky (excerpt) Submitted to the Dialog Area, Vietnam Protest, Nov. 27th, 1996
