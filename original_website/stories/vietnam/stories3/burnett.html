It's Thanksgiving Day 1996 and I'm going to spend the day with my family.

My high school friend Merlin Legeau won't be there.
He wasn't at the last one either.
He wasn't at our 30th high school reunion last year.
He wasn't at the 25th either.
He didn't have 4 kids.
He didn't start his own business.
He didn't get married.
He didn't move to New Jersey.

In fact, he wasn't even at graduation in '65.

He died in Vietnam in '65.

On this day we thank Merlin and all the others who gave their lives for us.

Glenn Burnett
Cedar Knolls, NJ
born 1947
