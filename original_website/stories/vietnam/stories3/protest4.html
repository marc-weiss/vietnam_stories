Jed Proujansky

I was clear that my "enemy" was not the soldiers, the grunts or "cannon fodder." My enemy was those who felt that the war was justifiable for economic and 'political' reasons. Now that I have three children, all of who are approaching the age where they may be faced with such choices I know that the main reason the war was wrong is because we did not value human life. Then I raged at the war, now I have learned to cry about the horror of it.
