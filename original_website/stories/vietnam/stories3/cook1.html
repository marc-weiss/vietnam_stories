Deborah Cook

Larry and I met when I was fourteen and he was sixteen. High school sweethearts, we became engaged when I was sixteen, just before he left for Viet Nam. I wrote a letter to him each day; sometimes more. He was shot in the leg in the 12th month of his 13 month assignment. He came home and during his convalescent leave, we were married. 10 months later our daughter, Laura, was born while Larry was in the Mediterranean. He left the Marine Corps shortly after his return.
