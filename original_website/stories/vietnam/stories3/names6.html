Michael E. Toliver

On the third day we pack up.... within minutes of our start, there is a single explosion. Someone has tripped a booby trap, and another name is ready for the wall in Washington.

Names appear on the wall in reckless profusion now. There is no leisurely "Thump!" as the booby trap goes off, the corpsman arrives, the medevac is called and ALFREDO MALDANDO appears on the wall.
