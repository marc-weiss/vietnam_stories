Why's that kid looking at me that way? Why did that woman change direction so quickly when she saw me? Why did that old man cross the bridge by my post 3 times in the last hour? Questions, questions questions...all day every day. Should I put my foot HERE or should I put my foot THERE? Answer the question wrong and you lose your leg, your nuts your life - maybe your buddy's life too.

Like I said, I got tons of questions. Problem is, I'm a little short on answers. If the Vietnam War can teach us anything, it can teach us that NONE of us has all the answers, not even you.

Mike Toliver (excerpt) Submitted to the Dialog Area, Vietnam Protest on Dec. 1st, 1996
