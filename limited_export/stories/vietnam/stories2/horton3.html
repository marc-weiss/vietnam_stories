Jerry Horton

I really enjoy meeting Vietnamese people who have migrated to the US. I like to talk to them about their country as it is one of the most beautiful places my eyes have ever seen. I've learned that we Vets really do not fit the stereotype. There are many of us on the left and the right but most of us are somewhere in the middle and we are rather normal. My son was born on veterans day and I like to think that he is my reward for going.
