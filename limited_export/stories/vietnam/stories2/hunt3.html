Joel Hunt Sr

I carried 113 dead Marines at Tam KY one day in May of '67. Most sickening site that I had, up until then, seen. My gunner threw up. The smell... what it meant...then prisoners were put on our planes to be taken out of the area and questioned. Imagine the little smart asses grinning. Defiant? No. Just scared and trying to be not-scared. A knife under their throats and the grins became tremblings.
