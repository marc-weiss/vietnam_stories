For those people who had lived during the war and the period after, please understand the innocent. We had left our homeland for a chance. A chance that we deserve just as much as anyone else. The War is over. Twenty years had passed and we should try to move on. It's terribly difficult to forget the past when you were in the damn situation, but political differences will only pollute your hearts.

To the soldiers : you have answered the call from your beloved country and you are true heroes. You saw the pain in the eyes of the children and you can still see that sadness in the eyes of my Vietnamese parents. Please try to understand.
