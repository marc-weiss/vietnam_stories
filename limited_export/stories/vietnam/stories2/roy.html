Mike Roy

Christmas of 1989, the last time my dad was at my house -- he died in 1990 -- he started carrying on about Vietnam vets, I guess they had some Vietnam vets at his VFW post that were pretty rowdy, a lot of these guys were bikers and what have you, and so Dad kind of looked at all of them that way and was making some derogatory remarks and I was sitting there and I go, "Dad, did you forget, I am a Vietnam vet?"
