Tiffany Truong

I was 3 when my family secretly boarded a boat to escape Vietnam. I still haven't been back since and do not know much of the logistics of the war. All I know is that my grandparents are still back there, of whom I've never met and my aunts and uncles are in the US. We were sent to Australia, where both my parents found work and walked one hour each way, every day as we had no money for a luxury car.
