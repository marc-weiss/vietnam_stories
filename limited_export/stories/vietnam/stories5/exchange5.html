Dr. Spatz,

Thanks for your kind words and for your offer of mutual respect.

I have noticed the last couple of years many veterans who have faced the reality which we all know - that is that there were things that deserved to be protested. It is the undeniable truth.

Also lately, I have had discussions with those who protested who have changed their way of thinking in terms of how we (those who served) are viewed.

It was not an easy time for any of our nation's young people. But, the exchange of words that we have had now are evidence that there is reason for optimism in the future.

May there never be another Vietnam.

Tom Dier
 submitted to Dialog area Vets and non Vets - a dialogue, Dec. 16, 1996
