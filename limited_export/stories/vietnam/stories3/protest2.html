PROTEST

Since I got back from 'Nam, I've spent a lot of time trying to make "sense" of the War. What I've found is that there were few real evil people - just people trying to muddle through as best they could, much like us grunts. What we need to do is acknowledge that the War was wrong, while recognizing the human failings that got us into it.

Mike Toliver (excerpt) Submitted to the Dialog Area, Vietnam Protest, Nov. 17th, 1996
