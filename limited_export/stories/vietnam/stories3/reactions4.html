There are numerous vets, myself included, whose friends, family, co-workers, etc. would probably be amazed to know that their vet was having any symptoms of PTSD. I doubt if anyone realized I was having any problems. Myself included.

Post Traumatic Stress Disorder is what you do to survive the events, the insanity. When that "event that is outside the range of human experience" is happening, it must be subdued, chained and locked away deep inside so you can survive and continue to function. Then later, it has to be dealt with in some way every day for the rest of your life.
