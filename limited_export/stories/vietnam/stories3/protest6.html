PROTESTER PONDERS

We all obviously have a lot to work out, twenty five years later. The saddest part of the whole experience is that the divergent camps never had a chance to talk about it until now! This dialogue is long overdue. We finally have a chance to face the raw emotion we all felt. These commentaries may not sway anyone out there, but they temper our own pain -- protester or participant.

Virginia (excerpt) Submitted to the Dialog Area, Vietnam Protest, Nov. 30th, 1996
