Vietnam was a major influence on my life...

As a young man I was called upon to serve my country just as my father before me...

I had no real understanding of the war or what it meant to my country or myself, I only had the understanding the news media had given us, and that was fairly distorted.

When I left for Vietnam I was 21 year old... when I returned I was 50...

For the first 15 years after I had returned from the war I had much anger in me that I could not resolve... This anger cost me dearly... lose of friends and family...

In the end the only demons I had to slay where the ones in my own soul... now I feel for the most part this is true with all anger... forgivness only comes from within, no one can forgive you for your sins, you must grow the strength to forgive yourself... and then you can accept the forgiveness of others.
