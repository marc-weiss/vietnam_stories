I'm a survivor. I do not know why. I was 18 when I went to Nam and spent 35 months there with the Navy doing Harbor Patrol. I met Nuygen Cao Ky once and learned the language . I learned not to hate as every man/woman are equal. I visited the Wall in 1990 and found it to be very healing and addicting. I spent 4 days in Washington D. C. and 35 months of nights. I feel like I belong on that wall but I know God has a purpose for me not being there he just hasn't shown me yet. Maya deserves the Freedom Medal for what she has done for our Veterans may God Keep her well. Thank you Maya.
