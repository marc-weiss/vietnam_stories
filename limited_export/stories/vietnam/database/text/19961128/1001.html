i remember when my cousin came home from vietnam. we all went to my aunt and uncle's house for dinner. i didn't know much about the vietnam war, except that some people didn't like the people who fought there. all night i wanted to tell my cousin "thank you" so he wouldn't think we didn't like him. but i was only twelve or thirteen and he was a grown up. i didn't say anything.

i didn't see him again for twenty-two or twenty-three years. when we met again i still wanted to tell him "thank you". i didn't - i was afraid and i don't know why.

i'll tell him now - thank you, Tom. love, julie
