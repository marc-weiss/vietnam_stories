I was born in 1965 but I was never too young to understand or not understand Vietnam. I have a 48 and a 46 yr.old sisters the oldest who had alot of contact with the men in Vietnam. I have read the letters she recieved, looked at the pictures. I new several people who came back from that war and some who did not. I have several friends that were there. I think that these boys who were sent there to come back troubled men was horrific, and for them to be shunned by fellow americans just baffles me. No one asked to go there! It was never an option, your name came up you went thats it. I am very proud of our Vietnam veterans and I fly my POW-MIA flag proudly, on a daily basis I contribute to the Disabled Veterans and think the government,ha,ha, should give these men alot more then grief, they deserve everything our outrageous government can offer. I Would just like to tell all the Vietnam Veterans Thankyou, I am proud of what you did for our country and my heart goes out to all of you.
