I am a Vietnam widow who was 22 years old and 7 months pregnant when my husband, Howard, was killed in action in Vietnam. My life and the life of my daughter were forever altered by his death. In recent years I have come to a reconcilation of the loss and am currently looking for opportunities to share what I've learned about war, grief, reconcilation and healing. My memoir, Grief Denied, A Vietnam Widow's Story, is a journey of healing and reconcilation. For more information about my story please visit my web site at www.griefdenied.com

Pauline Laurent
1945
