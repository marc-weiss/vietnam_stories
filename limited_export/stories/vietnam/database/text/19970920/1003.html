I am a twelve year old girl who has just been made aware of the many horrors of the Vietnam War...It scares the hell out of me how destructive war can be...There's also the not knowing...what happened to the men out there that still have not returned home...I attended the Vietnam Memorial day for the POWs and the MIAs last Friday at the New Jersey Vietnam Memorial...I was moved beyond tears...I wanted to just close my eyes and pretend it didn't happen...It made me so angry about how naive everyone is...how naive I was...I was a completely different person when I left that place...I felt the anger...the fear...the hope...I still do...I remember tracing every single letter of the men's names on the black shiny marble...carefully laying my head against the wall...wondering about...why did this have to happen??? Every night...I thank God that I was not here when it happened...I pray for all the families who have lost a loved one...I pray for all the people who had lived through that hell...I pray for all those whot have fought in a war to give me the life I have now...I pay my deepest respects to all you people... Love Always... Madrigalle
