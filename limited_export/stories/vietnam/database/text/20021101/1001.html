Vietnam was a tough time back in the good ole days. I was the general of the second battalion. We invaded on the vietnamese and snuck attacked them with hand grenades and pistols. The vietnamese did not see this coming, however one of our men tripped on a land mine and blew into 40000 pieces. It was a sad day when we would later lose 305 men. The second battalion had lost and I was the only remaining survivor. I was a POW for 3 weeks until they let me go. I stole an ak47 and shot 3 men in the face. I singlehandedly pushed the front into China, but it was too easy so I surrendered.

General Jeff Cryder
1940
