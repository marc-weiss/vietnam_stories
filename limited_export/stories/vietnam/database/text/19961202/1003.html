In '73, I was flying a F-4E Phantom, when tracers forced me to eject. I broke an arm and was captured. I stayed for 1 year and 3 months in Ha Noi. Me and another prisoner escaped one night and managed to get a distress signal out. 6 days later, We made it to the seaside, and were smuggled out on a South Vietnamese fishing boat. We eventually made to Ellis Island and were re-employed in less than a year.

Anomomous
1954
Chicago, Illinois
USA
