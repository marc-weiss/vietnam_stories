Poem

LOST

I'm lost between two worlds it seems, two worlds that don't agree. And lost between a world of dreams, and that of reality.

I'm lost between the things that were, and yet the things to be. And lost with in a timeless realm of souls that call to me.

I'm lost with in the things I say, and things I try to say. And lost because I can't express this pain you cannot see.

I'm lost because you cannot know these things I cannot say. And lost because you cannot feel these pains I hide away.

I'm lost with in the many faces of those who hide their shame, and those who would rather forget this war that brought such pain.

I'm lost with in this span of time, this horror of my day. And lost with in this realm of time that will never fade away.

By Fred Locke

Fred Locke
1947
