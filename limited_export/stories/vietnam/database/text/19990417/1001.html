I served aboard the aircraft carrier U. S. S. Coral Sea during the evacuation of Nam (Operation Frequent Wind) and the Mayaguez Rescue Operation in April and May of 1975. War is always a terrible thing and no one knows that like veteran. What is more terrible is when the sacrifice is wasted by those in authority. I'll always wonder whether we could've freed the Mayaguez hostages w/out landing the Marines on heavily defended Tang Island & I'll always wonder about the fitness of men like Bob McNamara, Gerald Ford, and Bill Clinton to send brave men like that to their deaths. I have no doubts about the honor and courage of those who bear arms in defense of our country. I salute all of our country's veterans but especially the heroes of Mayaguez. They are:

PFC Daniel Benedett PFC Lynn Blessing PFC Walter Boyd LCPL Gregory Copenhaver LCPL Andres Garcia HM1 Bernard Gause PFC Gary Hall LCPL Joseph Hargrove PFC James Jacques LCPL Ashton Loney HM Richard Manning PVT Danny Marshall PFC James Maxwell PFC Richard Rivenburgh SSGT Elwood Rumbaugh PFC Antonio Sandoval PFC Kelton Turner 2ND LT. Richard Van de Geer

These men, along w/ 58,000 other men on the Wall, were not babykillers. They were American heroes, the real deal. Sleep in peace and honor, gentlemen. A better day is coming. Sincerely,

Bill Both
