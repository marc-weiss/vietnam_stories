I was airlifted out of Vietnam in 1974 to England. I grew up in Leamington Spa, Warwickshire. I flew back to Vietnam in 1996 to make a Documentry for Network First on ITV. In 1997 I met Nicolaus Benjamin Trang Cleeve who was also airlifted out of Vietnam in 1974. The first time we met was at a Vietnamese reunion when I was twelve. In 1998 we married. When we have children they will be our first blood relatives that we will know of.

MAI-HOA CLEEVE
