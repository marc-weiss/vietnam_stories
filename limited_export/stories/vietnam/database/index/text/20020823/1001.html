I was born in 1965, when I was just 2yrs. old my father was off to vietnam,in August of 1967 he was killed.I have but one memory of him and it plays in my mind over and over.I often wounder how my life would be if he would have lived,I had an average childhood and was raised with a very loving stepfather who always had good things to say about my father even though he had never met him. I sometimes try to imagine what my father (E-8 Bert Howard Hinson )went through in the vietnam war or if he thought of his young daughter back in the states.I have tried to find family members or someone who might of had known him,just hoping that someone would be able to say " yes" your father loved you very much or yes he often spoke of his baby girl.I strongly believe that this war changed the lives of many who were too young to be at war, and there will always be unanswered questions. I named my first born after my father thinking that someday someone might be looking for family of Bert Howard Hinson.I am now 37 years old and still long for the father that I can not remember.

Cathelena Hinson Muniz
1965
