Friday, March 8th 2002

I am a junior in high-school. I am taking a college course class offered. It is an english class. One of our assignments was to find stories on the internet about the vietnam war to answer a question we had chosen a question that we want to find more about on the war) At first I was like ok this is dumb. Now that i have read almost all the stories on this site, I am actually interested in finding more about the war. I know that a lot f veterns have to go here to type all of the stuff about the war. I would like to say that I give you guys a lot of credit. i know how hard it is to talk about all of this stuff. I know someone who would hve nightmares during the night, if you woke him fast he would become violent... like he wold be when fighting a war. It is so scary. I am only 16 but the war has made a huge impact on my life. Whoever made this site.. Good job. And to all of the veterans... Thanks. Thanks for fighting the war. You are real heros.
