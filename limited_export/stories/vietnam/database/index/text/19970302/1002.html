I don't want this to be perceived as a commercial venture, but I do want my story out there. I have just finished a book, " hard to forget."

hard to forget is a book about a young Special Forces soldier who took his Green Beret to Vietnam in 1966, only to discover that the real war began when the fighting stopped. It is a book about forgiveness, friendship and survival. The combat scenes are real enough, but pale by comparison to the unwanted series of nightmares that struck fear into my heart for the next twenty years. Coming home required the reversal of irrational thinking, and my own need for forgiveness of self and others while trying to recover from the emotional scars of the deathlands.

hard to forget is a story about how one Vietnam vet tries to escape the ominous torments of war.

hard to forget will be published in October, 1997 - God willing, and the water don't rise.
