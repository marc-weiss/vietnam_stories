Dear All Vietnam Veterans,

My name is Missy and I am 16 years old. I am in a theatre arts group called The Colony Players, from The Colony, Texas. In October, the females in my theatre arts group will be performing a play called, "A Piece of My Heart". It is about 6 women in th Vietnam War. The 6 females go to the war looking for a way out, adventure, or for money, not knowing the hardships they would soon face in the future. Throughout the play, it shows the horrors they endured, and how it changed them, and their lives. The reason I have posted this note is to ask for all of your help. I want to know what it was like to be there. I know I can never even imagine what you all went through those long, terrifying years in Vetnam, but I want to make the best portrayal of The War that I can. I want the audience to be in tears by the end of our play, just like I was when I finished reading it. "A Peace of My Heart" was the most beautiful play I have ever read in my life. Please help me to show the world what you went through. Anything you could send me: pictures, letters, poems, etc- anything at all, would be greatly appreciated. Please.
