To the dad I never knew but somehow always had

Dad, I was too young. You didn't know I was on the way. When you found out, it was a letter too late and time has passed us by. Language kept you and mom apart. Pictures of you is all I have left. No name, just a face I have implanted in my mind. You were always here. I wonder If I was always there for u too? For what's it's worth, now 29 years later, I love you. -- MZ
