After 13 months in Nam as an Airborne Infantry soldier in Vietnam it was time for me to go home. I was so happy to have survived and couldn't wait to go back to the"WORLD".I couldn't wait to go to my own bed and be able to sleep a whole night in the security of my home. I just didn't realize that the greatest battle of mylife was just to begin. For 30 years I struggled with PTSD. I couln't sleep at night I had reaccuring nightmares. I would wake-up frighten. There was an anger in me that I couldn't shake off.There was no patriotic parade for us. No pat on the back"Great Job" instead you are a "Baby Killer". 30 years of misery, 30 years of insomnia. 30 years of rage,30 years of Hate. Relationships not working, in and out of jails for disorderly conduct. I wanted to just kill myself. Finally after a long struggle. I have overcome. "Greater is He then He that is in the World" I had a spiritual expierence and the chains of the PTSD bondage was broken by the man they called Jesus. God Bless you. The VA couldn't help me. Jesus has been mighty good to me. Jesus Loves me. Minister Jose Mireles 1/501 101 airborne Div. C co> from apr 69 to may 70
