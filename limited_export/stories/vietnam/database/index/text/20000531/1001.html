I wasn't alive during the time of Vietnam, but I have seen all the loss and pain that became of it. I never really understood why so many innocent people had to die, but I am able to except that they fought for what they thought was right. After all this time I wonder if people still remember and then think how could they forget. If I was alive during the time I would have been there right along side all the other protesters trying to make people see that war is not right, but did anyone ever stop to think about why the soldiers did what they did? I honor each one individually for their bravery and their courage. I only wish that I was that brave.

Erin Bishop
1983
