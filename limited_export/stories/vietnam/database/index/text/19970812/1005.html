I was a 2LT in April, 1966 and just finished 13 months in Korea as an
infantry platoon leader. I was assigned to the 9th Division at Fort Riley
Kansas and was assigned to an artillery battalion as a company commander
assigned to train recruits. My time was up in November of 1966 so the
pressure to reup was enormous. Every day some new colonel would visit me
to get me to reup. When they finally decided that would not work, I was
reassigned to train ROTC cadets for the Summer and then assigned as the
Chief of the Visitor's Bureau.

When I was at Fort Benning in November of 1964 going through IOBC, I met
several officers who I would run into at times during my tour. One I remet
at Ft. Riley but I can't remember his name - other than his first name
which was Bill. We used to drink "Wild Turkey" and listen to satirical
political records and the Smothers Brothers. He was "dating" the librarian
on the post at the time. He reupped and was assigned as the assistant to a
one star with the 9th. I would like someone to tell me his name or for
Bill to contact me. I think of him often because he made a difficult time
easy and made me laugh a lot. I have searched all of the wall sites but I
have not (thank God) found a name that would fit the time and place. Any
help would be appreciated.

Bill Draves
