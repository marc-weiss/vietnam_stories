I now believe, 32 years after receiving a near fatal head injury with shrapnel completely through the brain and loss of most of my right hand, that the only purpose for any war has been for research and development and population control. We have all believed a big lie from our politicians and educators, that we were saving our nation and the world. Only GOD can do that! The wars have been used as the tool to justify the destruction of almost every nation on earth and what has been achieved? Fewer and fewer people have gotten richer and richer.Yet for all the "wealth" I have never yet seen a hurse pulling a U-haul trailer. For those who have sent so many to their death or to be maimed for life, I wonder what we will say when we stand before our creator. There will be weeping, wailing, and gnashing of teeth! The truth WILL be made known.Perhaps later I can find my way back and tell the rest of MY story. I was a member of Foxtrot Company 2nd Battalion 3rd Marines 3rd Marine Division and was wounded on 5 May 1967 on hill 881 North[Khe Sahn].For the next war we should Force the politicians of the nations involved, to "lead" in every battle. Sincerely, J.A. Stone
