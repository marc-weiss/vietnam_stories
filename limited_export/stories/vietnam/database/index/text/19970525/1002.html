I served my country in Vietnam in 1969 and 1970 and I am sick to death of all the disfunctional losers who blame their drug dependence, homelessness, or other sad-ass story on having been sent to Vietnam. Having joined the gravy train of others who see fit to blame 'the system' for their own lack of drive, motivation, and ambition, they are unworthy of sympathy. As far as how it affected my life, my values, my relationships, politics, etc, etc... Not at all... Except that I refuse to watch any Oliver Stone movie. We were there for the right reasons. We were just not allowed to accomplish what we were sent there to do.

Ron Wheeler
1944
Wake Island, HI
