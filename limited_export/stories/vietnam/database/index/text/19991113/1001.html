It was my turn and was aware of being called when in school in 1967. I joined the USMC to the surprise of most that known me at the time. It was a turning point in my life that put me on a new path for my life as it is to day. Once out of Boot camp The our entire class was going to pull duty in Vietnam. I was an MOS of 3500 or Motor transport. I arrived in country on April of 1968. It was the last of the Tet and Kha Shan was still under Siege. I was with 9Th Motors attached to 3rd Marines and was in Dong Ha. Within a week or so My first big convoy was to relieve Kha Shan and though it was told that the battles where over that is not the case. I spent another teo Weeks under siege in Kha Shan and was one of the biggest battles I was directly involved with and pure hell. I was only their near the end and developed the famous stare that veterans get from that place real quick. I had felt from the time I enlisted that I might not survive the war and this experiance further reinforced that conclusion. Other than the Enemy trying to kill was Bombed with Agent Orange, and whatever else unknown to me and others at the time. My children from my first marriage where born with problems on dieing at 2 from comlications her Identical Twin now 29 but has had medical problems. My current kids when remarried in 1985 have had 3 more boys doing well seams only related to my just the period after just coming back. My attitude is depression but do not use the war as an excuse and try to control the thoughts or dwell on it much. But harder as I get older. I have avery liberal philosophy and makes my wife have problems with me as she don't fully understand my ways. I do not Drink or do drugs for I feel that it and truly does make for more problems. I use Swinging and open relationships as an out let to let out stress. Most of all the friends that where around are dead in the war or died of it upon return to the world or the US. I had plans to make a career in the service and was a pilot when I joined. The military at that time was the most screwed up and disorganized ever and in the wrong war which was not a war. Than anyother time in history. The veterans of that war that served the country because it was their duty to serve and had to suffer because of the leadership of the country through that time. I blame McCarthy the most on his fenatical hatred of Communism. It is nothing but an impossible form of govenment that is based on Humans being totally Equal and as we all saw it fell apart in 89 and the walls came down. A solder is the one who is most of all the one that hates war as we are the ones that put our lives on the line, but when we has to fight it is much better enjoy a victory!

Capt Gary Evans
