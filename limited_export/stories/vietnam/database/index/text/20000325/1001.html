I went to Vietnam in 1963, and served 2 tours there, I am 56 years old, I have four lovely children and have been through one marriage that was destroyed by the effects of my service in the war. I read the messages from all the young people wanting to know what it was like and how we felt, and what we thought. I Believed..I Trusted, and I was Betrayed. I cannot speak for anyone but myself. Vietnam happen so long ago for some people, but to me it happened YESTERDAY. It will never leave me, it colors my every thought, it has seared itself into my very soul it will be there forever. If I were given the chance to change it, go back and do it over, change things, my mind might scream yes, but my heart would say no. for all the reasons I am sitting here with tears falling down my face. It is the price I paid for being here. It was a charge aginst my ingorance, my lack of understanding of who my country was and what it was about. I love this country, it has made parts of my life hell,I have no other. I am like a man who loves dogs and is attacked by a very vicious one. He gets over the attack and he still likes dogs, but its not a thing he likes to talk about. Please be mindful of the world around you, it does not suffer a fool lightly.
