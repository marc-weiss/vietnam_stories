...Incident at the French fort… I was sitting up on top of one of those concrete bunkers the French had built when a loud scream caught my attention just as I pulled a can of peaches from my trouser leg pocket and started opening it with my p-38. I looked over to the ARVN side of the position, over by the flag pole, which was in front of the little building where the Die-we (sp?) had set up his HQ. A squad of ARVN were coming in from patrol. They had a couple of women with their hands tied behind their backs. They were shoving them towards the big, metal, water-barrels setting at the edge of their compound. I went ahead opening my can of peaches curious to see what the hell was going on over there when Holland came up and set down next to me. "Those mothers fuckers must be VC." He said. "Yea, What are they do'n?" "Interrogating 'em, I guess." "Yea. You got any pound cake?" "Naugh." I slurped a peach and I saw this ARVN officer take charge and twist up a stick with some barbed wire making a kind of bit and slipping it over one of the women's head. He twisted that sucker around and round until it was obviously digging into her face and the side of her head. The other women started screaming and kicking but they held her tight. Then, the soldiers all began talking, shouting really, at these two gooks. One of the soldiers grabbed the feet of the woman who had the wire bit in her mouth and tied her still. The kicking stopped but by now her head was getting damn bloodied up and it was running all down the front of her. " Sure you ain't got any pound cake?" I asked Roger. "Naugh man, really" Two of the soldiers picked the woman up, flipped her up side down, and stuck her head first into a barrel filled with water. Then, the officer took a stick the size of a ball bat and started beating the shit out of the barrel! Bam..bam..bam.. She was twisting, and turning, and flopping about so much the two soldiers almost dropped her completely in the barrel, but they held on while the officer kept pounding away. "Damn," Roger said, " Whyn't you save me one of those peaches?" "All yea man.Here, get ya one." When the soldiers pulled the woman out she was soaking wet, but it didn't wash the blood off because now it was pouring out of her ears, too. The other woman howlered and howled but, of course, neither Roger or I understood that gook jibberish. We just watched to see what was going to happen next. The officer shouted right into the woman's face. Then, she screamed back but... "Shit," Roger chuckled, "Wrong answer!" They picked up the woman with the bit in her mouth, twisted it a couple more times, and stuck her back in the barrel. The officer started banging the sides of it again, and again, and some more, and some more. Finally, somebody came out of the HQ and shouted something in that gibberish again,but it must have been an order cause they pulled the woman out of the barrel. They pushed and dragged the two women back to the HQ and disappeared inside. "Hell," I said, "I bet I got some pound cake down in the bunker. I'm gonna look." "Bring me some'n.back," Roger said. And that was it.
