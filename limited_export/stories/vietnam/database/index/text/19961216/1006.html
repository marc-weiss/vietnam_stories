I was a child during Vietnam, so I saw the dead, killed, given like baseball scores on the nightly news - enemy-dead and American-dead. And the Vietnam Memorial, finally, when it was made, was just another mindless, meaningless tabulation of Death. I think it's a hideous, terrible monolith -- something for people that can't commit themselves to an actual artistic statement. Especially in view of the fact that Americans killed weren’t even enough to fill a decent baseball stadium, and 2-million Vietnamese were killed. The overcompensation of Americans for their loss during Vietnam is appalling when you compare it to the devastation and genocide of the Vietnamese, the Cambodians, and Laotians. A hideous memorial to a hideous war!

Maxi Villasan
