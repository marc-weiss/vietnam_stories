I've got many stories. Some are funnier than MASH, others are sad. While the USA will never be the same because of the war, the men and women that SERVED and fought will also never be the same. Although that can be said of any war at any time in history. My heart aches for all those people, who's lives were tourn because if this conflict of political ideas. I believe I am a better person because of it. I am proud to have served my country and at the same time am ashamed of how our polititions lied to us about what the conflict was all about. For my fellow countryman who gave their lives in this conflict I cry but am also proud. For the politions that sent us to this needless battle . . . your souls will rot in hell.

Bill grey
1944
El Toro, CA USA
