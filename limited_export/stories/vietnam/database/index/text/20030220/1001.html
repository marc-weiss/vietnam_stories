I'm submitting this for my dad Louis Hansen who is no longer here with us. He passed away from cancer on March 25, 1995. I consider him to be a hero. I believe that every man and woman who joins the military is one. They are willing to sacrfice their lives in order to protect and keep all of us safe. My dad served two tours in the Vietnam War. The war changed every aspect of his life. He never did talk to me about what he experienced there, but he did tell my mom about how when him and his fellow soliders returned home they were spit on. I think that's really sad. These men were returning from hell, and instead of being supported, they were rejected.

Jennifer Hansen
1981
