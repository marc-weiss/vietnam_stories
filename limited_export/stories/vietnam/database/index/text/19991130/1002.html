I WAS DRAFTED 3 MONTHS AFTER MY 18TH BIRTHDAY AFTER TRYING TO AVOID THE DRAFT BY NOT REGISTERING. I HAD BEEN GOING STEADY WITH THE SAME GIRL FOR 4 YEARS, AND PLANNED TO MARRY HER WHEN I ESTABLISHED A VIABLE LIFE. HER FATHER WAS AGAINST OUR DATING SINCE I WAS SOMEWHAT LIBERAL AND HE WAS VERY CONSERVATIVE. THE LAST 2 YEARS OF OUR RELATIONSHIP SHE HAD TO SNEAK OUT TO SEE ME. WHILE IN VIET NAM I WORKED IN SEVERAL POSITIONS. MOSTLY I WAS WITH A 105 BATTERY AT VARIOUS FIRE SUPPORT BASES NEAR THE TEY NIHN AREA (5/68-5/69). I WAS ALSO WITH A F.O. TEAM. HAVING GONE THROUGH 3 F.O.'s, I SPENT ABOUT A MONTH ON MY OWN. AFTER 4 MAJOR GROUND ATTACKS AND SEVERAL AMBUSHES, I HAVE TO SAY THAT I'VE SEEN MORE THAN MY FAIR SHARE OF KILLING. BUT THE FEAR AND HATE I WAS EXPOSED TO THERE CHANGED ME. NOT INTO A HERO, BUT INTO SOMEONE WHO DIDN'T CARE ANYMORE. I LOST MY COMPASSION. UPON RETUNING HOME I FOUND THE HOUSE WHERE MY GIRL FRIEND LIVED WAS EMPTY. THEY HAD MOVED. WHILE IN VIET NAM HER FATHER HAD REMOVED MY LETTERS FROM THE MAIL AND DESTROYED THEM. I KEPT GETTING LETTERS FROM HER PLEADING FOR ME TO WRITE. WHEN I GOT HOME, I PROBABLY WOULD HAVE KILLED HER FATHER IF I COULD HAVE FOUND HIM, BUT THE FAMILY WAS GONE, AND EVEN FRIENDS OF HERS EITHER DID NOT KNOW OR WOULD NOT TELL OF THEIR LOCATION. SEVERAL YEARS LATER I RAN INTO HER AT A SHOPPING MALL. SHE FAINTED WHEN SHE SAW ME. IT SEEMS THE PAPER HAD RUN AN OBITUARY ON ME. SHE WAS NOW MARRIED AND HAD A CHILD. WE PONDERED WHAT TO DO. I DECIDED THAT TOO MUCH HAD HAPPENED TO REKINDLE THE RELATIONSHIP. I LEFT HER KNOWING THAT I WOULD ALWAYS LOVE HER. YOU MIGHT SAY I BECAME A YIPPY. I WAS HIGHLY IRRITATED WITH WHAT OUR GOVERNMENT HAD DONE TO ME. I WANTED TO CHANGE THINGS, AND WAS NOT ALL THAT NONVIOLENT IN MY FEELINGS. AFTER ALL I HAD LOST SOME GREAT BUDDIES IN VIET NAM AS WELL AS THE LOVE OF MY LIFE. I WOULD JOIN FORCES WITH VARIOUS GROUPS TO BRING THE GOVERNMENT BACK TO THE PEOPLE AND END THE IDIOT WAR. I HAVE STRUGGLED WITH AUTHORITY EVERSINCE. I HAVE HAD PROBLEMS WITH MY JOBS AND MY FAMILY. ALTHOUGH I HAVE SOUGHT HELP FROM THE VIET NAM OUTREACH PROGRAM, AND HAD MAJOR AFFILIATIONS WITH BOTH CHRISTIAN AND BUDDHIST GROUPS; AT THE AGE OF 50, I AM STILL LOOKING FOR INNER PEACE. I NOW DOUBT IF I EVER FIND IT. CHRIS COOK - rocknroll@harborside.com
