It is about my brother Jack. I saw him two weeks ago in Florence, Alabama. He is very near death now it seems with his liver. He returned from Vietnam with some honorrs, medals, and pride. We were proud of him. We thought of it as a good cause, America being there. Mom and Dad had four sons in the Army at the same time. Perhaps Jack being there kept others of us from the family from going. Like Jesus Christ taking on my sin so that I would not have to be judged as a sunner! Now Jack is very sick with that liver of his again. Worse now that it has been since he returned. Thank God, he returned. Help him one more time dear Lord to pull through this constant struggle with hepatitis and cirrhosis and a swollen pancreas and his memories and scars from "A WAR" faught in Vietnam.

Frank Prater
1949
