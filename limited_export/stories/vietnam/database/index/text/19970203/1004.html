Subject: drafted and still alive!

Comments for AT:

The boat left Oakland, went under the Golden Gate Bridge with 5,000 guys who didn’t give a shit. Two days out of Danang, 16 days later, the Mass held on one side of the boat was attended by most and tilted it. Two LST took us, divided to the sandy shore where the Marines waited , laughing. Few joined the laughter. The flares illuminated the sky and many of the clean green jungle fatigues got sandy. More laughter from the darkness toned, WELCOME TO THE 'NAM TROOP!

It rained for two months. In country training and two moves inland later, we settled into LZ Baldie. Captain Ebert would be dead in four month. Before, he read her letter, "I was in the kitchen killing rats and I thought about you". She was a stewardess for TWA. Brynathen being so close to Philly I felt close to the Captain though I was just a pounder of the keys and keeper of the paper. I, like everyone, respected him. Most of us were just visiting, raped and held hostage, but he and Top Soldier were living their life; until it ended.

Hatch

Bill Hatcher
