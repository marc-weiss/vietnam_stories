HOW AM I DIFFERENT TODAY AS A RESULT OF NAM. I TRUST NOONE BUT ESPICALY THOSE WHO TRY TO BE OVER ME IN ANY WAY. RELATIONSHIPS THATS A JOKE I'V BEEN MARRIED THREE TIMES AND RAISED FOUR KIDS. NONE OF WHICH I HAVE ANYTHING TO DO WITH.AS FOR MY PARENTS THERE DEAD NOW AND I NEVER WENT TO THE FUNERAL NOR HAVE I SEEN THEM SINCE COMING HOME FROM NAM.POLITICS I CANT STAND POLITICIANS,THERE ALL CROOKS.IF GIVEN THE CHANCE THEY WILL SUCK THE LIFE BLOOD FROM THE YOUTH OF THIS COUNTRY ALL FOR THERE OWN GREEDY NEEDS.ATTITUDE TOWARDS LIFE OR DEATH BRING IT ON.I MAY HAVE CANCER NOW AND I COULD GIVE A SHIT LESS. AS FOR PHILOSOPHY OF LIFE "IT DONT MEAN NOTHING." MY VALUES ARE I'M LIBERAL TOWARD THOSE THAT HAVE LESS THEN I DO. BUT CANT STAND THE WEALTHY,POWERFUL.I SPEND MY DAYS AND NIGHTS BY MYSELFE. NIGHTS BRING ON DREAMS,SWEATS,, NIGHTTERROR AND LITTLE SLEEP. I SHOP IN THE EVENING WHEN EVERYONE ELSE GOES HOME. HAVENT BEEN TO A MOVIE IN YEARS. I'V STOPPED DRINKING AND NO MORE DRUGS. I ALWAYS WONDER WHY NO ONE WRITE ABOUT TRUCK DRIVERS IN NAM? NOT ENUFF GLAMOR FOR ANYONE.DOES VIETNAM HAVE AN ENDURING MEANING IN MY LIFE.MY GOD WHO WRITE THIS STUFF? I CAN'T STOP THINKING ABOUT BUDDIES I LEFT THERE OR THE ONES THAT LEFT ME. ARE THEY OK, DID THEY MAKE IT OUT? THOSE THAT DIDNT MAKE IT OUT WHY CANT I REMMEMBER ALL THERE NAMES? WHY DID WE GO THERE, WHY DID WE GET TREATED THE WAY WE DID WHEN WE RETURNED? WHY DO POLITICANS INSIST ON SAYING EVERYTHING IS OK NOW THAT DESERT STORM WAS WON. I HAVE TO GO.
