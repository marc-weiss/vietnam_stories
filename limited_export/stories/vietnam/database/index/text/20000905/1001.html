I was like so many but never realized till years later only a part of me came home. I left a part of myself in Vietnam that has been trying to come home for 30 years. I always said those that died there was for a reason. Even if that reason is not known today by those that never went. Wars have never really been the main factor for the killing of people.The results of being in a war is what kills young men and women in their minds and body. If my going kept one other person from going then it was worth the effort.For I would not wish the experience of war on anyone.I am alive today but still have nightmares and cry when i see the flag burned by someone. Someone that is saying i have a right to do that because i am showing my anger against our government. Well that anger is against the people that have fought and died for that flag. To bad the young today do not take that in to concideration when they burn the flag or talk about doing it. It is true we did not win the war in Vietnam but from it maybe as a country we can learn not to repeat such an act on our young. I would go for my country if ask again but only would ask one thing this time give us a chance to win.Give us a chance to have our parade and be proud to march in the streets of our families.

Scott Black
1946
