Have been to Vietnam twice in last three years. Been fascinated with the culture and the history since I was a kid on Grandpa's lap watching Dan Rather in a flak jacket. Took my blond, blue-eyed twelve-year-old son to Hanoi and surrounding areas last summer; our most memorable experience was crossing the Long Bien Bridge at night, a remarkable feat of engineering and resilience. The friendliness is not all geared to getting the tourist buck. We would return tomorrow. And stay alot longer than our three weeks allowed us to. As Americans, we were welcomed with warmth and curiosity; we learned that we cultural interest beyond our bluejeans, which was nice. And, compared to the Vietnamese, Americans have alot to learn about recycling.

Peterson , Gordon
1962
