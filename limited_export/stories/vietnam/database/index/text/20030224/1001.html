My story begans many years ago. I was only 16 and he was 18. I was working at a small coffee house. this boy can in and ordered a cup of coffee. He was dressed in army greens. he asked if I could sit and talk with him. When my shift was up I walked with him to the port. We sat and talked for so long. It was like we knew each other. We wrote back and forth for a very long time. A year or so, but then one day he wrote and said," It is getting tough out here and I won't be able to write much. So don't worry." So when I went to a football game on a friday. I heard his name read on alist of Vietnam Dead. I cryed for what seemed like days. But then I meet my husband. Eric Scholer, I love him very much.

And now I know that no matter were I go to my guarding soldier will be right there!!!

Dixie Scholer
