Hey Folks, great site. Thought you might be interested to hear about an ongoing tribute to those who were held prisoner of war. The 560th Flying Training Squdron at Randolph AFB, TX continues to make the dream of one last flight a reality for many of our former prisoners of war possible. These men gather at Randolph for fun and remembrance, culminating in their "Freedom Flight", a final mission to complete the one on which they were shotdown and subsequently captured. At this date, over 183 former POWs have flown with us. March 13 & 14, 1998 will be the 25th reunion for these veterans and should be the finest gathering ever.
