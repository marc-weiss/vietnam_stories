Teresa Allen

My companion, a fellow worker, said, "Oh, I didn't know you knew anyone who was killed over there." "My brother," I managed to squeeze out before I erupted in what I can only describe as a fit of bottled up anguish that would not be denied further.

Once we found his name on the panel, I virtually collapsed in sobs and inexplicably longed for an eraser! God, how I wanted to take his name off that wall! I had so hoped that I wouldn't find it there.
