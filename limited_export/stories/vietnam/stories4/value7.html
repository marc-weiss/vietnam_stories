They no longer know what it is to work with others and feel within their own breast a corresponding spirit of respect. They have forgotten the sacred lessons of leadership . . . or, perhaps, they never learned those lessons in the first place. Perhaps they were too busy memorizing the maxims, too busy climbing the ladder. What is worse, they seem to know nothing of the legacies they leave behind, out of touch as they are with the world of the spirit.

Pat Hoy (excerpt)
NYC, NY
