Duane Hoffman

From 1967 to 1970, Bill enlisted in the United States Navy as a Seabee. He was responsible for defoliation of landing strips in Vietnam. Bill used and was exposed to Agent Orange for extended periods as a routine task. This chemical herbicide so damaged the stem cells in his skeleton that Bill was not able to produce red blood cells. He was not able to resist infections and died at the age of 39. He mercifully, left no heirs nor was he acknowledged for his sacrifice to the United States and the people of this country.
