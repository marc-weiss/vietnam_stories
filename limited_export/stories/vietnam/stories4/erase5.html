Joseph T Miller

THE GI ANTI WAR MOVEMENT

The reason no one *accurately* portrays the relationship between Vietnam veterans and the antiwar movement is because the people who continue to send our young men and women off to war need to erase that history. If young people knew that the antiwar movement during the Vietnam war was actually *pro-GI,* that would raise too many questions about the true effects of antiwar organizations such as ours, Vietnam Veterans Against the War, Inc.
