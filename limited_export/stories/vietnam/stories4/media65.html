Renny Christopher

I did not have much education when I began, having dropped out of college shortly after I started. I read. They were, overwhelmingly, books by white American men, because that was what I found in the bookstores and the city library. Eventually my life changed. I went back to college, then I went to graduate school and got an MA. My perspective had started to shift, because while teaching at San Jose State I encountered a large number of Vietnamese students.
