At 26, I am actually relieved to hear the word "Vietnam." It means that as a nation we've matured and we are finally willing to let some of the bitterness go. I think it began with Maya's solemn granite memorial--it reminded us that 57,000 people cannot simply be blotted out of our collective memory because they make us feel uncomfortable. It's about time we all started talking. The silence has lasted far too long.

Matthew Cardwell (excerpt)
Born 1970
