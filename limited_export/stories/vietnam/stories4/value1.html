Earl Martin

I worked in Vietnam five years during the war as a pacifist C.O., helping farmers who lost their homes because of the war. When the war ended in 1975, I stayed to continue our work with the Mennonites to clean up some of the unexploded munitions.

Since then, I have visited Vietnam three times, including "my" province of Quang Ngai where I visited the sad site of My Lai where so many farmers lost their lives on March 16, 1968
