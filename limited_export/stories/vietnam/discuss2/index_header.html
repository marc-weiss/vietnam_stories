The Forum
During a 2 1/2 week period in February and March, P.O.V. Interactive invited 15 people to join a private, moderated discussion to explore more deeply some of the issues that have been raised on these pages.

Forum participants debated issues of life and death, war and peace, conscience and loyalty from the perspectives of their personal experiences. Participants included Vietnam veterans, former anti-war activists, family members of soldiers who fought in the war, a Vietnamese refugee and a Mennonite conscientious objector. By the time it was all over, the group had generated more than 300 pages of writing on the legacy and history of the Vietnam War.

While you can't create new threads here, please feel free to add your comments to any of the topics below.
